2. 1.What is the cause of some fallen objects to slow downward?2.What would happen if there wasnt any air resistance to slow down falling objects?​


Answer:

What is the cause of some fallen objects to slow downward?

Air resistance (also called drag) slowed down the heavier piece. Drag opposes the direction that the object is moving and slows it down. ... If there were no air, the two objects would hit the ground at the same time. To slow down a fall of an object, you will want to create more drag.

.What would happen if there wasnt any air resistance to slow down falling objects?

​If there is no air resistance, after you let go of an object the only force on it is the gravitational force. ... More massive objects have a greater gravitational force. The acceleration of an object is proportional to the net force on the object and inversely proportional to the mass of the object.

8. even though jesus wasnt a king with an earthly kingdom, how did the people in Jerusalem show that they saw him as a king​


The people in Jerusalem saw Jesus as a king by laying palm branches and their cloaks on the ground as he entered the city, which was a gesture befitting a king's welcome. They also shouted "Hosanna to the Son of David!" which was a traditional greeting reserved for royalty. Additionally, Jesus' entry into Jerusalem on a donkey, a symbol of peace, was reminiscent of the prophecies of a coming king in Zechariah 9:9.

All about Auld Lang Syne

Filipinos really love to sing! They can pretty much sing anything or any song in any celebration. But in new year celebration they love to sing particularly this song because they can relate to the songs lyrics.

The song titled "Auld Lang Syne" signifies new beginnings that is why Filipinos think it's appropriate for the new year.

The song also reflects on old experiences from the previous year, old families and friends that still gathers for the special event and they celebrate and get ready for a new start.

17. 4n=2n+6 please help me i wasnt foucesd


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

4n = 2n + 6

Transpose

4n - 2n = 6

2n = 6

Divide the whole equation by 2

n = 6/2
