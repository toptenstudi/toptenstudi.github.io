13. why contemporary arts are conceptual, innovative, expressive and experimental?​


Answer:

Contemporary art is often characterized by being conceptual, innovative, expressive, and experimental due to several reasons:

~Reflection of the modern world: Contemporary art often reflects the rapidly changing and complex nature of the modern world. Artists seek to explore and express the complexities, challenges, and nuances of contemporary society, culture, politics, and technology through their conceptual and innovative approaches.

~Emphasis on individual expression: Contemporary art encourages individual expression and the exploration of personal ideas, concepts, and perspectives. Artists strive to convey their unique vision and engage with the audience in novel and thought-provoking ways, resulting in innovative and experimental art forms.

~Breaking traditional boundaries: Contemporary artists often challenge traditional artistic boundaries, norms, and conventions. They may experiment with different materials, techniques, styles, and formats, pushing the boundaries of what is considered "acceptable" in the art world and creating new forms of artistic expression.

~Conceptual focus: Conceptual art, a prominent movement within contemporary art, prioritizes the idea or concept behind the artwork over the physical object itself. This emphasis on ideas, concepts, and intellectual exploration leads to the creation of art that is often thought-provoking, challenging, and innovative in its approach.

~Engaging with diverse perspectives: Contemporary art seeks to engage with diverse perspectives, voices, and identities. It often addresses social, political, cultural, and environmental issues, pushing boundaries and provoking critical thinking. This emphasis on expressing diverse viewpoints and exploring new ideas can result in conceptual, expressive, and experimental forms of art.

~Evolving technology: Contemporary art is influenced by the rapid advancements in technology, which provide artists with new tools, mediums, and opportunities for experimentation. Artists may incorporate digital media, interactive elements, and multimedia approaches in their work, resulting in innovative and experimental forms of artistic expression.

Explanation:

contemporary art is often characterized by being conceptual, innovative, expressive, and experimental due to its reflection of the modern world, emphasis on individual expression, breaking of traditional boundaries, conceptual focus, engagement with diverse perspectives, and the influence of evolving technology.
