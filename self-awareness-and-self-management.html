23. Assess yourself .How can you apply the key 4 skills for building your EQ to yourself? Explain your answer.1.Self management2.Self awareness3.Social awareness4.Relationship managementpa answer plss​


Answer:

Here's how I would apply the key 4 skills for building emotional intelligence (EQ) to myself:

Self management: To practice self management, I would focus on regulating my emotions and behavior in a healthy and productive way. This could involve setting goals for myself, managing my time effectively, and practicing stress management techniques such as deep breathing or meditation.

Self awareness: To develop self awareness, I would try to become more attuned to my own emotions and the ways in which they influence my thoughts and actions. I would also work on understanding my strengths, weaknesses, and values, and how they shape my perspective and behavior.
