Answer:

½

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1 : Make 6 balls and 12 balls a fraction

Step 2 : Simplify the fraction 6 / 12

6 ÷ 2 = 3
12 ÷ 2 = 6

3 ÷ 3 = 1
6 ÷ 3 = 2

The simplified fraction is ½

Step 3 : Write the final answer ½
