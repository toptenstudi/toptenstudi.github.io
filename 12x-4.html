14. 1. 3x(12x + 4) = 36x2 + 12x​


Answer:

It is called distributive property

Step-by-step explanation:

This is easy to remember, if you recall that "multiplication distributes over addition".

Step 1: First, multiply the 3x to 12x to get 36x^2 (3x12=36) (x^1 * x^1 = x^2, here the two exponents which is 1 and 1, we just add them to get the exponent 2) (so we get 36x^2)

Step2: Do also in 4, multiply 3x to 4 to get 12x ( here we don't need to multiply anything the x of 3x because the 4 have no x, so we now have 12x)

Now we get the 36x^2 + 12x

Formally, they write this property as "a(b + c) = ab + ac".
