Frozen speech is used generally in a very formal setting and does not require any feedback from the audience.

It is the most formal communicative style that is usually used during respectful events and ceremonies.

Formal speeches are straightforward speeches. In this speech style, the speaker avoids using slang terminologies; what the speaker says is something that has been prepared beforehand.

Casual

This is informal communication between groups and peers. Casual style is used in conversation between friends and insiders who have something to share and have shared background information but don’t have close relations.

Consultative

Intimate
