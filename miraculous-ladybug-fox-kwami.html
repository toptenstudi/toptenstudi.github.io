Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir is a French CGI action/adventure animated series produced by Zagtoon and Method Animation, in association with Toei Animation, SAMG Animation, and De Agostini S.p.A. The series features two Parisian teenagers, Marinette Dupain-Cheng and Adrien Agreste, who transform into the superheroes Ladybug and Cat Noir, respectively, to protect the city from supervillains which are created by the villain Hawk Moth. It airs in over 150 countries, each with its own order of episodes; this list of episodes will follow the airing order in France.

In the United Kingdom and Ireland, the show premiered on 30 January 2016 on Disney Channel.
