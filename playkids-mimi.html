21. mimi baked chocolate cake.she gave 2/7 of cake to elaine and 1/5 to melinda.what part of the cake was left to with mimi​


Answer:

18/35

Step-by-step explanation:

You just need to add the fraction of cake that Mimi gave to her friends and subtract it from the whole cake.

Since the fractions are dissimilar or having different denominators, we'll make them similar first.

2/7 = 10/351/5 = 7/35

Solution:

[tex]1 - ( \frac{10}{35} + \frac{7}{35} ) \\ = 1 - ( \frac{17}{35} ) \\ = \frac{35}{35} - \frac{17}{35} \\ = \frac{18}{35} [/tex]
