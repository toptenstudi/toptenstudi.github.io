2. what are the 3 kinds of distortion produced during the welding process​


Answer:

Longitudinal shrinkage - This type of distortion occurs perpendicular to the weld and runs horizontally through the cross-section of the weld.Transverse shrinkage -  This type of distortion occurs parallel to the weld and runs vertically through the cross-section of the weld.Angular distortion - the distortion is transverse to the welding direction and caused by shrinking near the fusion zone resulting in a change in the angle of the parts.
