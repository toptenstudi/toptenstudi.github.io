1. 1.What are cohesive divices? 2.What are the common function of the different. cohesive divices? ​


Answer:

1.What are cohesive divices?

A cohesive device is a word or phrase that helps to connect two ideas or sections of writing. Cohesive devices can be divided into two categories: lexical cohesive devices and grammatical cohesive devices.

2.What are the common function of the different. cohesive divices?

The most common function of cohesive devices is to provide cohesion, or flow, between ideas in a text. This can be done in a number of ways, such as through the use of pronouns, repetition, and transitional words and phrases.
