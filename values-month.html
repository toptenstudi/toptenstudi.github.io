1. At the start of the month, the value of an investment was $48.45. By the end of the month, the value of the investment changed by a lost of $13.80. What was the value, in dollars, of the investment at the end of the month?


Answer:

$34.64 for end of the month

Step-by-step explanation:

start of the month = $48.45

$13.80 = lost during the end of the month

$48.45 - $13.80 = $34.65

end of the month = $34.64

10. Claim of policy and claim of value fire prevention monthconversatiom​


Claim of policy:
Person "Did you know that October is National Fire Prevention Month?"
Person B: "Really? What does that mean?"
Person "It's a month where people are encouraged to take steps to prevent fires in their homes and communities."
Person B: "That sounds important. What kind of steps can we take?"
Person "Well, we can make sure to check our smoke detectors, have fire extinguishers on hand, and create an escape plan in case of a fire."
Person B: "I think everyone should be required to do these things to keep themselves and others safe."

In this example, Person B is making a claim of policy by suggesting that everyone should be required to take steps to prevent fires.

Claim of value:
Person "October is National Fire Prevention Month. It's a great time to remind people about the importance of fire safety."
Person B: "I'm not sure it's that important. Fires don't happen that often."
Person "Actually, fires can happen more often than you might think. And even if they don't happen often, the consequences can be devastating. That's why it's so important to take steps to prevent them."
Person B: "I guess that makes sense. I just think there are other things that are more important to focus on."
Person "I disagree. I believe that preventing fires is a fundamental value. We need to protect ourselves and our communities from this kind of danger."

In this example, Person A is making a claim of value by arguing that preventing fires is a fundamental value that should be prioritized.

25. php13,523 rate is .32% in every 3 months. in 3 years and 9 months what is the interest and tghe future value


Answer:

Interest = 64,910.4

Future value = 78,433.4

Step-by-step explanation:

Convert 3 years to months

3 × 12= 36

36months + 9months = 45 months

* it increases .32% every 3 months

so you need to divide 45(months) into 3

→45 ÷ 3 = 15

Then,

15 × .32 = 4.8%

13,523 × 4.8 = 64, 910.4( interest )

13,523 + 64,910.4 = Php78,433.4( future value )
