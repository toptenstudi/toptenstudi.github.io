12. It is required for DNA and RNA synthesis and for protein synthesis and cell development.​brainliest ko po kayo


Like DNA >> RNA >> Protein.

Protein synthesis is one of the prime processes happening constantly in a cell in any organism. It is central to biology. Without proteins, we would not be able to survive. Even the smallest organisms like bacteria and viruses need proteins to survive. The mechanisms may differ extensively amongst organisms but the essentiality of the process is constant.Protein specific genes are transcribed to mRNA in nucleus, that mRNA migrates to the cytosol. rRNA then, with the help of tRNA, synthesize protein according to the mRNA code.

DNA → RNA → Protein : this is the central dogma of molecular biology.

DNA (the gene sequence) tells the sequence which has to be copied, RNA is the image of DNA which is copied to synthesize (Translate) a protein.

DNA to RNA to Protein.

The sequences are:

At first DNA the transcription.By transcription, the DNA produces mRNA.Next this mRNA is grabbed by the ribosome sub-units and tRNA comes to start the process translation.Through translation, protein is produced.So ultimately, DNA & RNA both are acting together to produce protein.
