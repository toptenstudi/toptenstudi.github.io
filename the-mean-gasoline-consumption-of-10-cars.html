2. a car travels 282 km and uses 30 liters of gasoline. the cars rate of gas consumption is?


Answer:

the car's rate of gas consumption is 9.4 km/liter.

Step-by-step explanation:

The rate of gas consumption can be calculated by dividing the distance traveled by the amount of gasoline used:

Rate of gas consumption = Distance / Gasoline used

In this case,

Rate of gas consumption = 282 km / 30 liters = 9.4 km/liter

Therefore, the car's rate of gas consumption is 9.4 km/liter.
