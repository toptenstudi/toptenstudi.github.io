STRUCTURES OF REPORT

A structures of report is similar to that of an essay: it has an introduction, a body, and a conclusion. In business, when employees are faced with a problem, the reporting structure of the company might help them figure out who to contact. Employees can comprehend various components of a business, such as communication, decision-making channels, and position interrelationships. The organization, on the other hand, must make the reporting structures obvious in order for employees to understand how to follow protocol.

The interrelationship between several authorities in a firm is referred to as a reporting structure. This is a command structure that specifies who reports to whom. In small organizations with a few employees, some reporting systems are self-evident. The objective of a company's reporting structure is to inform employees on where and how authority flows within the company. It defines the roles of various personnel, including the manager or managers to whom they must report and the departments to which they are assigned. Employees with functional competence can sometimes be in positions of responsibility despite not being in a managerial position.

But why do we need to improve our English skills? In the past decades the use of English language continuously to be in demand because of the growing economy, for example learning how to make a good academic text that will surely get the attention of your target reader is not an easy task because your goal is to provide the information they needed while entertaining them to read more, it is like creating a well crafted story that will surely catch your reader off guard but that story you created should only be based on facts and those facts should come from credible sources. Academic text is not only about writing but you can also present it orally by means of explaining it to your class or telling them story about the text you wrote in this way students  will not be just be student they can be teachers too because you teach or share the valuable information you shared. Learning EAP can greatly boost your confidence in front of people since you know what you are saying and you  can also impress the audience.

EAP can also help in the students not just by improving their English skills but also it can helps the students to learn and increase their knowledge about their goal because teachers can instruct their students to create an academic text . For example the teacher learn that that his/her student will pursue a biology course, the teacher will instruct to create an academic text about biology and present it to the class this way the students can learn additional knowledge about biology and also enchance their skills in creating a good academic text. EAP teachers can organize micro-conference  in which the students can talk about the about the topic they are given and prepare information that will be valuable in the topic the are given. An important component of EAP is helping the students to see how academic genres are used, it can boost the students analyzing skill in which students can examine the audience, purpose, grammar, meaning and language use in the text to gain an understanding of how these elements contributes to the text structure.
