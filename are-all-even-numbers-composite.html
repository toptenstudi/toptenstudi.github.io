Answer:

There are 12 divisors of 60 namely 1,2,3,4,5,6,10,12,15,20,30, and 60.

a. There are 3 prime numbers which are 2,3, and 5. Therefore, the probability is 3/12 or 1/4 when simplified.

b. There are 8 composite numbers which are 4,6,10,12,15,20,30, and 60. Therefore, the probability is 8/12 or 2/3 when simplified.

c. There is only 1 multiple number which is 60. Therefore, there is a 1/12 probability.

d. There are 8 even numbers which are 2,4,6,10,12,20,30, and 60. Therefore, the probability is 8/12 or 2/3 when simplified.

e. There are 4 odd numbers which are 1,3,5, and 15. Therefore, the probability is 4/12 or 1/3 when simplified.
