7. What are alternate interior and alternate exterior angles?​


Answer:

Angles that are in the area between the parallel lines like angle 2 and 8 above are called interior angles whereas the angles that are on the outside of the two parallel lines like 1 and 6 are called exterior angles. Angles that are on the opposite sides of the transversal are called alternate angles

Step-by-step explanation:

Alternate exterior angles are the pair of angles that are formed on th outer side of the parallel lines but on the opposite side of the transversal.

Alternate interior angles are formed by a transversal intersecting two parallel lines . They are located between the two parallel lines but on opposite sides of the transversal, creating two pairs (four total angles) of alternate interior angles. Alternate interior angles are congruent, meaning they have equal measure.

18. 1.Inday and bebang are comparing their heights.if Inday's height is 120¾cm and bebang's height is 96⅓cm.what is the difference in their heights​


Answer:

Asked: It was asked the difference of the height of Inday and Bebang.

Given: Inday's height was 120 ¾ cm while Bebang's height was 96 ⅓ cm.

Operation: Subtraction

Number Sentence: 120 ¾-96 ⅓=N

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution:

120 ¾-96 ⅓

=120 9/12-96 4/12

=24 5/12

Complete Answer: The difference in their heights was 24 5/12 cm.

Checking:

1.

24 5/12+96 ⅓

=24 5/12+96 4/12

=120 9/12

=120 ¾

2.

120 ¾-24 5/12

=120 9/12-24 5/12

=96 4/12

=96 ⅓
