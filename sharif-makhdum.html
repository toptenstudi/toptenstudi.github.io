1. ano ang kahulugan ng sharif makhdum​


Answer:

Si Karimul Makhdum ay isang Arabong mangangalakal na dumating sa katimugang Pilipinas noong 1380 at nagtatag ng moskeng Sheik Karimal Makdum sa Simunul, Tawi-Tawi na pinakamatandang moske sa Pilipinas

Makhdum Karim or Karim ul-Makhdum was an Arab Sufi Muslim missionary from Arabia who came from Malacca.[1] Makhdum Karim was born In Makdonia,[2][page needed] him and the Wali sanga were affiliated with the Kubrawi Hamadani missionaries in the late 14th century. He was a Sufi who brought Islam to the Philippines in 1380, 141 years before Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan arrived in the country. He established a mosque in Simunul Island, Tawi Tawi, Philippines, known as Sheik Karimal Makdum Mosque which is the oldest mosque in the country.
