30. 9. Which of the following tells that light travels in a straight line? a. Light travels in different directions. b. Light travels in a straight line in all situations. c. Light travels in a straight line until it hits something. d. All of the above.Pls po I need it rn.. ​


Answer:

C.

Explanation:

Light Travels In A Straight Line Until It Hits Something

Answer:

The correct answer is 'c. Light travels in a straight line until it hits something.'

When light travels through a uniform medium (such as air, vacuum, or a transparent material), it travels in a straight line. This is because light waves are electromagnetic in nature and they travel at a constant speed in a straight line until they hit an obstacle or change medium.

Option a, "Light travels in different directions," is incorrect because light waves always travel in a straight line unless they are refracted or reflected by a medium or surface.

Option b, "Light travels in a straight line in all situations," is also incorrect as light can be refracted or reflected when it encounters a different medium or surface.

Therefore, option c, "Light travels in a straight line until it hits something," is the most accurate statement.
