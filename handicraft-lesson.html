Answer:

Definition of 'handicraft':

A handicraft is a piece of art or a decorative item that is made by hand, using simple tools and materials.

The three basic elements of handicraft are:

1. Material: This is the type of material that is used to make the handicraft. Common materials used in handicrafts include wood, metal, glass, and fabric.

2. Technique: This is the way in which the material is worked to create the final product. Common handicraft techniques include carving, weaving, and painting.

3. Product: This is the final outcome of the handicraft, which can be either functional or decorative.

Some examples of handicrafts that I am interested in are:

1. Woodworking: This involves working with wood to create furniture, sculptures, or other decorative items.

2. Metalworking: This involves working with metal to create jewelry, tools, or other functional items.

3. Glassworking: This involves working with glass to create stained glass windows, vases, or other decorative items.

28. Relate Cambodian handicrafts to Philipine handicraft give 5 example and explain the relations​


Answer:

The history of Cambodian art stretches back centuries to ancient crafts. Traditional Cambodian arts and crafts include textiles, non-textile weaving, silversmithing, stone carving, lacquerware, ceramics, wat murals, and kite-making. Beginning in the mid-20th century, a tradition of modern art began in Cambodia, though in the later 20th century both traditional and modern arts declined for several reasons, including the killing of artists by the Khmer Rouge. The country has experienced a recent artistic revival due to increased support from governments, NGOs, and foreign tourists.
