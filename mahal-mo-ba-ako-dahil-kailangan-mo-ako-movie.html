21. Explain in English"Wag mo akong mahalin dahil mahal kita,Mahalin mo ako dahil mahal mo ako"​


"Wag mo akong mahalin dahil mahal kita,Mahalin mo ako dahil mahal mo ako"

In English translation, this means "Don't love me because I lòve you, lòve me because you love me too."

EXPLANATION:

This line means that the speaker wants to be loved not by force. The speaker wants to feel love that is genuine and not because his partner doesn't want to hurt the speaker's feelings. In Filipino, walang napipilitan. What the speaker only wants is true love where there is no one being forced to love the other and the feeling is mutual.
