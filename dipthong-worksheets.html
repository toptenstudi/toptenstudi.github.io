3. what is the meaning of dipthong


A diphthong is a sound made by combining two vowels, specifically when it starts as one vowel sound and goes to another, like the oy sound in oil. Diphthong comes from the Greek word diphthongos which means"having two sounds." Notice the di- for "double."
