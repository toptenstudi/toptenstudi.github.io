7. Peter bought a high-end laptop computer that costs 65000 pesos on installment basis. He paid 20000 pesos down payment. The remaining balance was paid in 36 monthly installment basis at 15% compounded monthly. How much is the monthly payment.


65,000 minus 20,000 = 45,000 ÷ 36 =1,250 is the monthly payment


The formula for calculating the fixed loan installment P is

[tex]P=\frac{Lq^n(q-1)}{q^n-1}[/tex]

where:

L = value of the loan (in this question L = 65000 - 20000 = 45000)

n = number of loan repayments

q = 1  + m,  where m = y / (12 * 100)  is mouthly interest rate in the form of the number (in our question yearly interest rate y = 15%)

[tex]q=1+\frac{15}{12\cdot100}=1+\frac{1}{80}=\frac{81}{80}\\\\P=\frac{45000\cdot(\frac{81}{80})^{36}\cdot\frac{1}{80}}{(\frac{81}{80})^{36}-1}=1559.94\ pesos[/tex]

24. The payment by installment are done periodically , and in equal amounts. This payment scheme is called?


Explanation:

What Is an Installment Debt?

An installment debt is a loan that is repaid by the borrower in regular installments. An installment debt is generally repaid in equal monthly payments that include interest and a portion of the principal.

This type of loan is an amortized loan that requires a standard amortization schedule to be created by the lender detailing payments throughout the loan’s duration.

KEY TAKEAWAYS

—An installment debt is a loan that is repaid in regular installments, such as most mortgages and car loans.

—Installment loans are good for borrowers as it's a way to finance big-ticket items, while they provide lenders with regular payments.

—Installments loans are generally less risky than other alternative loans that do not have installment payments, such as balloon-payment loans and interest- only loans.
