21. finleys pumpkin had a mass of 6.5 kilograms before he carved it. After carving it, the pumpkin has a mass of 3.9 kilograms. what was the percent decrease in the mass of the pumpkin?


Answer:

40 %

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the percent decrease, use the formula

% decrease = (amount of decrease)/(original amount)

The amount of decrease in the pumpkin is

6.5 - 3.9 = 2.6

The original amount is 6.5.

This gives us 2.6/6.5 = 0.4 = 40%
