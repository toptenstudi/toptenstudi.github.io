(a) 40 minutes

40 minutes is a fraction of the first two hours therefore the payment is P20.

(b) 3 hours

The first 2 hours cost P20 per hour and the remaining hour cost P10

The payment therefore is P20(2 hours) +  P10 (1 hours) = P50

(c) 150 minutes

150 minutes is equivalent to 2.5 hours

Dividing into two. 2 hour and 30 minutes

Therefore the payment is 20 x 2 hour= P40  + the payment of 30 minutes which is 10 =  P50
