NUMBERS OF ENROLLEES THIS YEAR BY KEY STAGES

The table shows the total number of students enrolled by Key Stage.

Firstly, There are 3,833,280 students registered this year. There are 874,199 students were enrolled in private schools. There seem to be 194,185 pupils in Kindergarten through Grade 3, 136,897 children in Grades 4 through 6, 272,157 Junior High School students, 270,022 Senior High School students, and 932 students with special needs.

Secondly, There are a total of 2,947,310 students who attend public schools, divided into Key Stages 1 and 2. Each grade has 1,036,525 students and 782,627 students enrolled. The remaining Key Stage 3 and 4 stages, as well as Learners with Special Needs from all Key Stages, have a scattered population of 180,695 and 10,173, correspondingly.

Thirdly, SLUS/LUCs have a total of 11,771 students enrolled. There really are 872 kids in Kindergarten through Grade 3; 824 students in Grades 4 through 6; 3,462 students in Junior High; 6,613 students in Senior High; and no special needs students.

Finally, 1,231,582 Kinder to Grade 3 kids, 920,348 Grade 4 to 6 students, 1,212,909 Junior High School students, 457,330 Senior High School students, and 11,111 Learners with Special Need pupils are named.
