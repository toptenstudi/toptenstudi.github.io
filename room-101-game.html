To compute for the Room Occupancy Percentage (ROP) of Amaya Hotel and Restaurant, we need to know the total number of rooms in the hotel and the number of occupied rooms.

Total number of rooms = 4 (Rooms 101, 102, 201, and 202)

Number of occupied rooms = 3 (Rooms 101, 201, and 202)

ROP = (3 / 4) x 100%

Activity 4B:

To compute for the Total Room Sales (TRS) of Room 101 in Nocete's Hotel, we need to know the rate per room and the number of times it was occupied.

Rate per room = Php 350.00

Number of times Room 101 was occupied = 3

Therefore, TRS = rate per room x number of times occupied

TRS = Php 350.00 x 3

TRS = Php 1050.00

So the Total Room Sales of Room 101 in Nocete's Hotel is Php 1050.00.

24. PLZ HELP The rooms along one side of a hotel corridor are numbered 101, 102, 103, ... , from east to west. Continuing with consecutive integers, the rooms along the other side of corridor are numbered west to east. There are the same number of rooms along each side of the corridor. If room 111 is opposite room 128 how many rooms are along the corridor?


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's assume that there are 'n' rooms along each side of the corridor.

We know that the room opposite to 111 is 128. Since the rooms are numbered consecutively, the difference between their numbers is the same as the number of rooms between them. Therefore, the distance between room 111 and room 128 is 128 - 111 - 1 = 16.

Since there are 'n' rooms on each side of the corridor, there are a total of 2n rooms. We can divide these rooms into two groups of n rooms each, one group on each side of the corridor.

The distance between room 111 and the end of its group of rooms (i.e., room 101) is 111 - 101 = 10. Similarly, the distance between room 128 and the end of its group of rooms (i.e., room 2n) is 2n - 128 = n - 128 + 111 = n - 17.

We know that the distance between room 111 and room 128 is 16, and this distance can be expressed as the sum of the distances between room 111 and the end of its group of rooms, and between room 128 and the end of its group of rooms:

16 = 10 + (n - 17)

n = 23

Therefore, there are 23 rooms on each side of the corridor, and a total of 46 rooms along the corridor.
