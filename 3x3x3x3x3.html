EXPONENTS ACTIVITY

1.)  [tex]3\times3\times3\times3\times3[/tex]

⇒ [tex]3^{5}[/tex]

2.) [tex]6\times6\times6\times6\times6\times6\times6[/tex]

⇒ [tex]6^{7}[/tex]

3.) [tex]4\times4\times4\times7\times7[/tex]

⇒ [tex]4^{3}\times7^{2}[/tex]

4.) [tex]7\times7\times 9\times 9\times9\times12\times 12\times12\times12[/tex]

⇒ [tex]7^{2}\times9^{3}\times12^{4}[/tex]

5.) ten squared

⇒ [tex]10^{2}[/tex]

How did I get the answer?

Simply get the base number, and count how many it was repeated. Here is the example:

[tex]5\times5\times5[/tex]

1. Just copy the number 5.

[tex]5[/tex]

2. Count how many 5 was repeated.

There were three 5s.

3. Make the number 3 as a superscript to number 5.

[tex]5^{3}[/tex]
