29. Danny has 48 boxes of face masks. He shared 3/4 of it to his friends. How many boxes of face masks did he shared?


Answer:

36

Step-by-step explanation:

48÷4=12

12×3=36

This is easier to understand if you'll draw a pie and cut it into 4 equal parts (48 ÷4). In doing so, the 48 as a whole pie will now have 4 equal parts with the 12 as the value of each. The next thing you want to do is shade the 3 parts (3/4) of the pie. Add the value of the shaded parts to get the answer (12+12+12 or 12x3).
