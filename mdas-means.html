19. What does mdas and gmdas mean?


MDAS stands for "Multiplication Division Addition Subtraction" which simply means to perform the indicated equation using Multiplication as first step.
GMDAS means "Grouping Multiplication Division Addition Subtraction" which simply means to perform the indicated equation using Groupings as first step.

21. what is the meaning of mdas


✏️ Answer ✏️

Multiplication, Division, Addition, and Subtraction

MDAS stands for Multiplication, Division, Addition, and Subtraction. It is part of the Order of Operations, a collection of rules that gives a sequence for simplifying mathematical operations. It is used when an expression or equation has more than one operation.
