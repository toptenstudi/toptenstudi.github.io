Answer:

V ≈ 9236.28

Step-by-step explanation:

Use the formula for finding the volume of a cylinder: V = π[tex]r^{2}[/tex]h

Write all the given.

h = 15 cm

r = 14 cm

Substitue the given to the solution.

V = π ([tex]14^{2}[/tex]) (15)

Simplify the exponent,

V = π (169) (15)

Now, multiply.

V = π 2940

The final answer is:
