From the 1950's onward, handbags began to gain cult status.

High-end design houses began to release “It” bags, classic styles that would eventually become iconic. The first was Chanel's 2.55, named for it's release date of February 1955 (and incidentally, has enjoyed a resurgence in the 2000's).
