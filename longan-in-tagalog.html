7. a kitchen is 4 and 1/3 longan 2 and 3/4 m wide how many square meters of linoleum are required to cover the floor​


Answer:

It requires 11 m² of linoleum

Step-by-step explanation:

FORMULA: A=l×w

A= 4 1/3 × 2 3/4.

(change first into improper fraction)

= 13/3 × 11/4

(multiply both the numerator and denominator)

= 132/12

(change the improper fraction into mixed fraction by dividing the numerator into the denominator)

(If you can divide it without remainder, you will put the whole number)

= 11 m²
