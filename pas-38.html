38=21+2w by 1/2

Divide 2w by 1/2 to get 4w

38=21+4w

Swap sides so that all variable terms are on the left hand side.

21+4w=38

Subtract 21 from both sides

4w=38-21

Subtract 21 from 38 to get 17.

4w=17

Divide both sides by 4.

w=17/4

w=17/4=4 1/4=4.25
