1. how to insert a audio in PowerPoint​


Answer:

In normal view, click the slide that you want to add sound to. On the Insert tab, in the Media group, click the arrow under Audio. In the list, click Audio from file or Clipart audio, locate and select the audio clip that you want, and then click insert. The audio icon and controls appear in the slide.

10. how to insert audio in ms powerpoint​


Answer:

Add audio from your PC

1.Select Insert > Audio.

2.Select Audio on My PC.

3.In the Insert Audio dialog box, select the audio file you want to add.

4.Select Insert.

Record audio

1.Select Insert > Audio.

2.Select Record Audio.

3.Type in a name for your audio file, select Record, and then speak.

Note: Your device must have a microphone enabled in order to record audio.

4.To review your recording, select Stop and then select Play.

5.Select Record to re-record your clip, or select OK if you're satisfied.

6.To move your clip, select and drag the audio icon to where you want it on the slide.

7.If you’re using more than one audio file per slide, we recommend putting the audio icon in the same spot on a slide to find it easily.

8.Select Play.

19. how are you going to insert a music in Microsoft PowerPoint​


Answer:

In the file explorer, locate the music file you want to use and then select Insert. With the audio icon selected on the slide, on the Playback tab, select Play in Background.
