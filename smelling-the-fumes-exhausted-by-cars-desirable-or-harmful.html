Desirable and Harmful Actvities

1. Placing the biodegradable wastes in the compost pit

This is a desirable activity. Biodegradable wastes are those wastes that decay over a period of time. A compost pit, on the other hand, is a dug area in the soil where biodegradable wastes are placed. Over time, these wastes in the compost pit are converted to organic materials and eventually will be converted to nutrient-rich material. Thus, in the process of adding biodegradable wastes in the compost pit, there will be more sources of organic materials thus, improving the quality of soil and growth of plants.

2. Smelling the fumes exhausted by cars

This is a harmful activity. Fumes from cars are one of the causes of air pollution. It is considered harmful and can be deadly to the body. It contains carbon monoxide which definitley negatively affects the body. Inhaling this carbon monoxide can be poisonous to the body which may cause death. Carbon monoxide clings or attaches better to the blood's hemoglobin, thus, instead of distributing oxygen to the cells, the carbon monoxide are being transported. The body requires oxygen for the cells to function well and once the supply is not enough, it affects the functioning of the cells and the body in general. Also, aside from carbon monoxide, the fume also contains other carcinogens which negatively impact our health. Fumes of cars are also harmful to plants and animals.

3. Using broken glass to cut paper

This is a harmful practice. Even though the broken glass and scissors are both sharp, the pair of scissors is safer to use. The pair of scissors / cutter are designed to cut.They have set of handles that will allow cutting easier and at the same safe to use.  This handle will protect one's hand from sharp areas. However, if we use broken glass, the risk of hurting ourselves will be high.

4. Covering the textbook with acetate

This is a desirable practice. As one covers the textbook with acetate, the book is more protected. Since acetate is water-proof, it can protect the book from the liquid that may tear the pages. Thus, the acetate will help in prolonging the book.

5. Burning plastic bottles​

This is a harmful practice. As you burn plastic, we pose a threat to plants', animals', and man's health in general.  It releases toxic gases that are dangerous or harmful to the health when inhaled. Specifically, the substances released into the atmosphere when inhaled can increase the risk of respiratory and heart ailments. the substances produced contribute also to air pollution and climate change.
