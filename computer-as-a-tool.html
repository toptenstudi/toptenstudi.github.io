12. computer hardware servicing tools


Computer Hardware Servicing Tools

Computer hardware servicing tools are essential for repair and maintenance of the hardware components of computers. These tools are important to take care of a computer, do the necessary troubleshooting whenever a computer is having issues, and perform computer upgrades. Computer hardware servicing tools are categorized into electrostatic discharge tools, hand tools, diagnostic tools, and cleaning tools.

Categories of Computer Hardware Servicing ToolsElectrostatic discharge tools - prevents or regulates the static electricity between two objectsEx:Anti-static wrist strapAnti-static matHand tools - tools that are operated by hands and without the use of electricity or motorsEx:Screwdrivers such as Torx, Philips head, and Flat headNut or Hex driverFlashlightPart retrieverTweezersNeedle-nose-plierWire cutterDiagnostic tools - used to locate and identify problems in the hardwareEx:Loopback adapterMultimeterCleaning tools - for removing dirt trapped in the hardwareEx:Cable tiesLint-free clothCompressed air
