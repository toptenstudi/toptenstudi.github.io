1. lanny walks around a square garden .if one side measure 40 m what is the distance covered by lanny


Answer:

160m

Step-by-step explanation:

If one side of the square garden is 40m, and assuming that Lanny walked on the perimeter of it, by formula of perimeter of square which is:

P = 4s

P = 4(40m) = 160m

The distance traveled by Lanny, assuming that she only revolved once, is 160m.
