17. What makes us yawn? Yawning is something that we cannot control. Even in the mother's womb, eleven-week-old babies have been observed to yawn. But why do we do it? One popular explanation for yawning is that a person may be tired or bored. Although many believe this to be true, it cannot explain why athletes yawn before an event or why dogs yawn before an attack.
