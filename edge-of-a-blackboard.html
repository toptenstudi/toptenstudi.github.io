19. A rectangular blackboard is twice as long as its width. There is small concrete that surrounds the blackboard. The concrete that surrounds the blackboard is a constant 4 cm wide and has an area of 334 square centimeter. Find the dimension of the blackboard.​


Answer:

L=22.5cm, W=11.25cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Concrete:

Thickness =4

Side1=8+2W

Side2=2W

Blackboard:

A=8(8+2W)+8W

A=334

W=11.25

L=2W

L=22.5
