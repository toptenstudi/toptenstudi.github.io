7. In Las Vegas, stores charge a 4.6% gst. Yuki is purchasing a handbag priced at $220 before tax. How much does she need to pay for the handbag? Can you guys know the answer of this?


18.15

Step-by-step explanation. Percent means per hundred, so we can convert 4.6% and 3.65% to equivalent decimals. 4.6%= 4.6 divided by 100 = 0.046 3.65%= 3.65 divided by 100 = 0.0365 Since both sales tax rates apply to $220, we can add the two rates. 0.046 + 0.0365= 0.0825 0.0825 x 220 = 18.15 And so, Yuki pays$18.15 in sales tax for her handbag purchase.

The amount of sales tax that Yuki needs to pay for her handbag is $18.15.

Step-by-step explanation:

220 × 0.0825 = 18.15

The amount of sales tax for her bag is $18.15.
