Square Root of Numbers

The square root of numbers pertains to a value that when multiplied by itself, the answer is the original number. On the other hand, most numbers are non-perfect square. This means that their square roots are decimal numbers, instead of whole numbers.

Going back to the first question above, you have to find in which two consecutive integers the square root of 77 lies. Below are the steps to find the answer:

1. Find the nearest lower perfect square number.

The nearest lower perfect square number to 77 is 64.

2. Give the square root of the nearest lower perfect square number.

The square root of 64 is 8.

3. The two integers are the square root of it and the next number to it.

The two integers are 8 and 9.

The square root of 77 lies between 8 and 9.

Then, for the second question, you have to choose the number which is not a perfect square. To find the answer, get their square root and the one with the decimal number is the answer.

a. √121 = 11 ✖

b. √132 = 11.49 ✔

c. √169 = 13 ✖

d. √196 = 14 ✖

132 is not a perfect square.

25. what is the number that when added to its square,the result will be 132​


Answer:

The number is either -12 or 11.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's say that the number is x

[tex]x^2+x=132\\x^2+x-132=0\\(x+12)(x-11)=0\\x+12=0;x-11=0\\\boxed{x=-12};\boxed{x=11}[/tex]

Checking:

[tex]x=-12\\x^2+x=132\\(-12)^2+(-12)=132\\144-12=132\\132=132\\\\x=11\\x^2+x=132\\(11)^2+11=132\\121+11=132\\132=132[/tex]

30. One number is the square of another. Their sum is 132. Find the numbers


Answer:

11 and 121

Step-by-step explanation:

express the question mathematically..

one number is the square of another = x^2

so that gives us x and x^2

their sum is 132 = x^2+x

now we simplify by making sure all elements of the equation onto 1 side for easier solving.

x^2+x-132=0

then we factor out this polynomial :)

(x-11)(x+12)=0

now we equate each factor to zero...

x-11=0

x=11

x+12=0

x=-12

we have 11 and -12, but the negative result should not be used since we need a positive sum :)

x=11; therefore the square of 11 is 121

then substituting these results to check if we got the right answer,

is 132 equal to 11+121?
