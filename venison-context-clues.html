15. meaning of the following: 1.sloppily 2.pestle 3.venison 4.marrow 5.expulsion in a sentence


1. sloppily - adverb from the adjective sloppy, which means untidy, with lack of neatness.
I don't believe that you're such a bungler! How can anyone live so sloppily?

2. Pestle - as a noun: a tool for grinding of mashing the substances. As a verb: to grind, to mash. 
In old times, the pestle was used for grinding. 

3. Venison - a meat gained from a flesh of the deer or other game animal. 
My grandfather made an excellent steak from this venison.

4. Marrow is a fatty tissue in the cavities of bones.

5. Expulsion is an act of expelling. 
He was threatened with an expulsion from the university.
