7. 2. The numerator of a fraction is 5 less than the denominator. If the fraction is equivalent to1/2, find the fraction.​


Answer:

5/10

Step-by-step explanation:

If “The numerator of the fraction is five less than the denominator,” then you could write the denominator as x and the numerator as x-5.

That means that the missing fraction is (x-5)/x.

According to the question, this is equivalent to 1/2

Therefore, we can set up an equation:

(x-5)/x = 1/2

To solve, cross multiply:

2 (x-5)=1 (x)

Solve for x:

2x-10 = x

2x - x = 10

x = 10

Remember, this is only x. You must still plug it into the fraction:

(x-5)/x = 1/2

(10-5)/10 = 1/2

5/10 = 1/2

Answer: 5/10


8. If the numerator of a fraction is increased by 2, the fraction is 1/4.If the denominator is decreased by 6, the fraction is 1/6. Find thefraction​


Answer:

The fraction is 1/12.

Step-by-step explanation:

Numerator: x

Denominator: y

Equation (1):

(x+2)/y = 1/4

y/4 = x+2

y = (4)(x) + (4)(2)

y = 4x + 8

Equation (2):

x/(y-6) = 1/6

y-6 = 6x

y = 6x + 6

Equate the two equations in terms of x. Solve for numerator x:

4x + 8 = 6x + 6

6x - 4x = 8 - 6

2x/2 = 2/2

x = 1  

Numerator : 1

Find the denominator y:

y = 4x + 8 = 6x + 6

y = 4(1) + 8 = 6(1) + 6

y = 12

Denominator: 12

The fraction: 1/12

Check:

The fraction = 1/12

If the numerator of a fraction is increased by 2, the fraction is 1/4.

(1+2)/12 = 3/12 = 1/4  (True)

If the denominator is decreased by 6, the fraction is 1/6.

1/(12-6) = 1/6  (True)

15. the sum of two fractions is 13 1/2.if one of the fractions is 7 1/5,what is the other fraction?​


Answer:

6 3/10

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the other fraction.

We know that the sum of two fractions is 13 1/2, which can be written as 27/2 in improper fraction form.

So, we can write an equation:

7 1/5 + x = 27/2

First, we need to convert 7 1/5 to an improper fraction:

7 1/5 = (5*7 + 1)/5 = 36/5

Now we can substitute this into the equation:

36/5 + x = 27/2

To solve for x, we need to get it by itself on one side of the equation. We can start by multiplying both sides by 10, which will eliminate the fractions:

10(36/5 + x) = 10(27/2)

72 + 10x = 135

Next, we can isolate x by subtracting 72 from both sides:

10x = 63

Finally, we can solve for x by dividing both sides by 10:

x = 63/10 = 6 3/10

Therefore, the other fraction is 6 3/10.
