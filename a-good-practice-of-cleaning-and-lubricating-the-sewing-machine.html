1. cleaning and lubricating the sewing machine at least _________ is a good practice.​


Answer:

once every week

Explanation:

All dust should be removed from the exposed parts at least once every week, and the important parts of the machine should be oiled.


2. what good interval in cleaning and lubricating the sewing machine??​


Answer:

Answer: It is a good practice to oil the machine after each day's work or after 8 to 10 hours of use. Even if you do not use your machine often, oil it occasionally to keep the oil from drying and gumming.
