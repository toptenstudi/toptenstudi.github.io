Explanation:

1. Buyayang, buyayang' is a Butuanon folk song about a water strider. Gary Granada reworked the lyrics of the folk song to come up with a composition that illustrates the impact of violent conflict on Mindanao communities, in particular, the lumad communities. Unlike the water strider that hardly breaks the surface of water as it walks across it, violent conflict - as Gary Granada, Bayang Barrios and Aliah Dimaporo lament - all too easily breaks the stillness of rural communities, especially in Mindanao.
