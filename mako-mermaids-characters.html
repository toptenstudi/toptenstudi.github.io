4. the sum of patrick's age and makos age is 58.eight years ago patrick was twice as old as mako then how old is mako


Present ages:
Patrick's age: x
Mako's age: y

Equation 1:
x + y = 58
y = 58 - x

Ages 8 years ago:
Patrick's age: x - 8
Mako's age: y - 8   or  58-x-8    or 50-x

Solution:
x - 8 = 2 (50-x) 
x - 8 = 100 - 2x 
x + 2x = 100 + 8
3x = 108
3x/3 = 108/3
x = 36  

Substitute 36 to x in Equation 1:
x + y = 58
36 + y = 58
y = 58 - 36
y = 22

Present ages:
Patrick: x = 36 years old
Mako: y = 22 years old

ANSWER:  Mako is 22 years old at present.

Check:
Present ages:
36 + 22 = 58
58 = 58

Ages 8 years ago:
Patrick: 36 - 8 = 28 years old
Mako: 22 - 8 = 14 years old

Patrick's age then was twice as Mako's:
28 = 2 (14)
28 = 28

Mako's age is 33 years old.

Further explanation

Solving Linear equation from Word Problems:

To solve the given problem, we must know how to construct word problems into the form of a linear equation or any type of equation.

Here are the steps:

Read the problem carefully to find the key details.Define descriptive variables to represent the quantities.Denote by the variables as x, y, etc.Convert the problem into mathematical terms or mathematical statements.Identify a solution method from the provided information to what we are trying to find.Solve the equationMake sure the solution satisfies the requirements of the problem by checking.

GIVEN PROBLEM

The sum of Patrick's age and Mako's age is 58. Eight years ago, Patrick was twice as old as Mako.

Question

How old is Mako?

Solution

Let:

P = Patrick's ageM = Mako's age

Let's translate each phrase or sentence from the given question into a mathematical expression or equation:

The sum of Patrick's age and Mako's age is 58.

P + M = 58     (1)

Eight years ago, Patrick was twice as old as Mako then.

P - 8 = 2 × (M - 8)

Expanding

P - 8 = 2M - 16

Add 8 on both sides

P - 8 + 8 = 2M - 16 + 8 P = 2M - 8      (2)

Substitute equation (2) for equation (1)

P + M = 58(2M - 8) + M = 58 3M - 8 = 58

Add 8 on both sides

3M - 8 + 8 = 58 + 83M = 66

Divide both sides by 3

M = 22

Then, Mako's age is 33 and Patrick's age is

P = 2M - 8P = 2(22) - 8P = 44 - 8 P = 36.
