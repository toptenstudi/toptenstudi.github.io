1. What are the key words in comparing and contrasting basketball and soccer?


Answer:

Compare and Contrast Paragraph Example

Basketball and Soccer

Basketball and soccer are sports with many similarities and

differences. Both sports are fun to play and each one has many

beneficial qualities. Here are some of the similarities and differences

with basketball and soccer.

Basketball and soccer are played with a round ball. They both

provide a ton of cardiovascular exercise such as running; so if you want

to get in shape these are the sports to play. A referee judges each

game. When a player breaks the rules he or she is called for a foul. A

soccer player receives a red or yellow card and a basketball player is

called for a foul. Finally a score is kept in each game. The team with

the highest score wins.

Even though basketball and soccer are similar, differences do

exist. Even though an athlete dribbles the ball in both sports, a

basketball player uses his hands and a soccer player uses his feet.

Usually soccer is played outside on a large field and basketball is played

indoor on a court made out of wood. Basketball is played with 5

players and soccer has 11. Lastly, basketball has four quarters and

soccer has two halves.

In conclusion both sports are fun and exciting to play. Each one

offers exercise and competitiveness. Also, they are unique in their own

way.

The difference is the Basics. Differences exist between soccer and basketball, including ball types, field dimensions and scoring. 13. 1. which is best to play basketball or soccer why ? 5pcs2. which is more famous, soccer or basketball ? 5pcs​


Answer:

1. Choosing between basketball and soccer depends on an individual's personal preference and physical abilities. Both sports have their unique benefits and challenges. Basketball requires quick reflexes, jumping ability, and teamwork. Soccer, on the other hand, demands endurance, agility, and skillful footwork. Ultimately, the best sport to play depends on what an individual enjoys and feels comfortable with.

2. In terms of global popularity, soccer or football is more famous than basketball. Soccer is considered the most popular sport in the world and has a massive following, particularly in Europe, South America, and Africa. Basketball, on the other hand, is popular in the United States, China, and the Philippines. While basketball has a significant global following, it doesn't have the same level of global popularity and cultural significance as soccer.
