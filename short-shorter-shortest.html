18. the longest side of a triangle is thrice the shortest side and the third side is 2 cm shorter than the longest side. if the perimeter of the triangle is at least 68 cm find the minimum length of the shortest side..send help huehue mamayang 5 na Yung pasahan​


Answer:

the shortest side = 10 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Formula for the perimeter of a triangle = sum of all sides.

Given: perimeter is at least 68 cm,

the longest side is thrice the shortest side(bale po yung hypotenuse is 3 times na mas mahaba sa shortest side)

the third side is 2 cm shorter than the longest side( bale po hypotenuse minus 2 po).

so yung ginawa ko po is nag trial and error.

nag-isip po ako ng number na kapag dinivide niyo sa 3 at mininusan niyo ng two tapos pinagsama-sama niyo, 68 makukuha niyo, so triny ko po yung 30,

30(hypotenuse{the longest side}) divided by 3 = 10(shortest side)

30 - 2 = 28(second to the longest side)

10 + 28 + 30 = 68

68 = 68(which is the perimeter)
