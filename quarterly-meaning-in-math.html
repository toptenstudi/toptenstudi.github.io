7. Quarterly payments of P2000 for 5 years with interest rate of 8 % compounded quarterly.​


Given:

R = P2000m = 4im = 8% = 0.08j = 0.08 / 4 = 0.02t = 5 yearsn = (m)(t) = 4 × 5 = 20

Using the formula

Maturity value (F) = [tex]\rm R[ \frac{(1 + j) {}^{n} - 1 }{ j} ][/tex]

Present Value (P) = [tex]\rm \frac{F}{(1 + \frac{im}{m} ) {}^{mt} } [/tex]

Solving for Maturity value

[tex]\rm F = R[ \frac{(1 + j) {}^{n} - 1 }{ j} ][/tex]

[tex]\rm F = 2000[ \frac{(1 + 0.02) {}^{20} - 1 }{0.02}][/tex]

[tex]\rm F = 48594.74[/tex]

Solving for present value

[tex]\rm P = \frac{F}{(1 + \frac{im}{m} ) {}^{mt} } [/tex]

[tex]\rm P = \frac{48594.74}{(1 + \frac{0.08}{4}) {}^{20} } [/tex]

[tex]\rm P = 32702.87[/tex]

Therefore, the maturity value is P48594.74 and the present value is P32702.87.

12. quarterly payments of 3000 for 7 years with interest rate of 8% compounded quarterly ​


Answer:

To calculate the total payment for quarterly payments of 3000 over 7 years with an interest rate of 8% compounded quarterly, you can use the following formula:

Total payment = Payment * ((1 + i)^n - 1) / i

Where Payment is the amount of each payment, i is the interest rate per period, and n is the total number of periods. In this case, the total payment would be calculated as follows:

Total payment = 3000 * ((1 + 0.08/4)^(4*7) - 1) / (0.08/4)

This gives a total payment of $34632.63. Note that this calculation assumes that the interest is compounded quarterly, so the interest rate is divided by 4 to convert it to the interest rate per quarter.
