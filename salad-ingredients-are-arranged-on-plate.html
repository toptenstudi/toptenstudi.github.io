8. Salad ingredients are arranged on plate rather than being mixed togethera . Fruit saladB. Composed saladC. Bound saladD. Vegetable salad


(B) Composed Salad is the salad whose the ingredient are arranged and not mixed together.

Explanation :

Salads are generally healthy and low-calorie foods that contain fruits or vegetables mixed together. Over time, salads are also given additional mayonnaise sauce or cheese sauce to add to the taste of the salad. However, there is one type of salad that is quite different from salads in general, namely composed salad.

The composed salad contains the same ingredients as the usual salad ingredients, such as vegetables and fruits. The difference lies in how the salad is served. If an ordinary salad generally mixes all the ingredients evenly, in a mixed salad, they arrange them so that each ingredient is arranged into several parts and is not mixed. The composition of the salad presents a faster form and is more suitable for eating more than one person in one serving.
