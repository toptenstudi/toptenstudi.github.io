9. Identify the parts of the circle. Write the answer on the blank after the number, is the center of the circle. is the chord of the circle. and are the diameter of the circle. 4 are the radil of the circle.​


Answer:

Is the center of the circle?

Fixed Point

A circle is defined as the locus of a moving point on a plane such that its distance from a fixed point on the plane remains constant or fixed. That fixed point is called the center of the circle.

Is the chord of the circle?

Line Segment

A line segment that joins two points on the circumference of the circle is defined as the chord of the circle. Among the other line segments that can be drawn in a circle, the chord is one whose endpoints lie on the circumference.

Are the diameter of the circle?

Longest Chord

The diameter of the circle is called longest chord.Example: AB is the diameter of the circle, which is called as longest chord.

Are the radii of the circle?

Radius

Radius of a circle is the distance from the center of the circle to any point on it's circumference.

13. Define the following :1. Secant of A circle 2. Tangent of a circle 3. Segment of a circle 4. Sector of a circle ​


Answer:

1. Secant of a circle: A line that intersects a circle at two points.

2. Tangent of a circle: A line that touches a circle at exactly one point.

3. Segment of a circle: A part of a circle that is bounded by an arc and its endpoints.

4. Sector of a circle: A region of a circle enclosed by an arc and two radii.

29. The radius of a circle is 4 feet.What is the area of the circle?​


Answer:

The area of a circle can be calculated using the formula A = πr^2, where A is the area and r is the radius.

In this case, the radius is given as 4 feet. Therefore, the area of the circle is:

A = π(4 feet)^2

A = π(16 square feet)

A ≈ 50.27 square feet

So the area of the circle is approximately 50.27 square feet.
