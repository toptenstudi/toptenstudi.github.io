1. K5 Learning Rectangles - area and perimeter Grade 4 Geometry Worksheet Find the perimeter and area of each rectangle. 2. 1. 3. 11 in 5 in 21 yd 5 yd 12 Yo 16​


Answer:

To find the perimeter and area of each rectangle, we can use the formulas:

Perimeter = 2(length + width)

Area = length × width

Using these formulas, we can calculate the perimeter and area of each rectangle:

1. Rectangle with length 11 inches and width 5 inches:

Perimeter = 2(11 + 5) = 2(16) = 32 inches

Area = 11 × 5 = 55 square inches

Therefore, the perimeter of the rectangle is 32 inches and the area is 55 square inches.

2. Rectangle with length 21 yards and width 5 yards:

Perimeter = 2(21 + 5) = 2(26) = 52 yards

Area = 21 × 5 = 105 square yards

Therefore, the perimeter of the rectangle is 52 yards and the area is 105 square yards.

3. Rectangle with length 12 yards and width 16 yards:

Perimeter = 2(12 + 16) = 2(28) = 56 yards

Area = 12 × 16 = 192 square yards

Therefore, the perimeter of the rectangle is 56 yards and the area is 192 square yards.
