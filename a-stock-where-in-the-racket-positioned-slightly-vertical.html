Explanation:

Also called in a different name of 'Ping Pong'or 'Whiff-whaff'.

The goal of the game is, the two-player needs to hit the tennis ball using

a small type of tennis racket. It is being played in a flat table divided into two equal sides with the use of a net which is set at the middle.

Here are the answers to the puzzle block:

1. RECEIVE- is a type of stroke to the response to the serve.

2. BLOCK- is a stroke wherein the Table Tennis Racket is positioned in a

slightly vertical,

 which is used to hit or strike the tennis ball on

the lower part of the Table Tennis Racket that makes the tennis ball move

in a straight pattern.

3. PUSH - is a short stroke.

It is also known as a type of underspin stroke which is almost close to the side of the

net. It is a way of stroke whenever there is no possibility to make an attack.

4. DRIVE-  a stroke wherein the racket is moved forward and upward at an angle.

These are the other term used for this stroke; Counter, Counterdrive, and Smash.

5. SERVE- a stroke that starts with a rally.

these strokes can be done either with forehand or backhand.
