9. 32. Angelika hiked 4 3/8 miles one day and 2 5/16 miles the next day. how many miles did Angelika hike in all​


Answer:

She hiked 6 11 /16 miles.

Solution:

4 3 /8  + 2 5 /16

= (4 + 2) + (3 /8  + 5 /16 )

= 6 + 3 × 2 /8 × 2  +  5 /16

= 6 + 6 /16  + 5 /16

= 6 + 6 + 5 /16

= 6 + 11 /16

= 6 11 /16
