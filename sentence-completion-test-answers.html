10. In 50 item math test, jazzmine got 98% of correct answers how many items did she answer correctly? Understand asked: Given: Number Sentence: Solution: Complet Answer:


Answer:

Understand: We are given a 50 item math test and Jazzmine got 98% of the correct answers. We need to find out how many items she answered correctly.

Given:

Number of items in the math test: 50

Jazzmine's percentage of correct answers: 98%

Number Sentence:

Let x be the number of items Jazzmine answered correctly.

Then we can write the following proportion:

x/50 = 98/100

Solution:

To solve for x, we can cross-multiply the above proportion:

x * 100 = 50 * 98

x = (50 * 98) / 100

x = 49

Therefore, Jazzmine answered 49 items correctly out of the 50 item math test.

Complete Answer:

Jazzmine answered 49 items correctly out of the 50 item math test.
