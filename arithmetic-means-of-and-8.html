28. If three arithmetic means are inserted between 8 and 16,find the second arithmetic mean


The second arithmetic mean is 12.

Step-by-step explanation:

8, ___, ___, ___, 16

To find d, substitute the first and the last term on the rule:

an = a1 + (n-1)d

16 = 8 + (5-1)d

16 = 8 + 4d

16 - 8 = 4d

4d = 8

d = 2

So to find the means, add the preceding numbers by 2, simultaneously. That is:

8 + 2 = 10

10 + 2 = 12

12 + 2 = 14

Thus, the second arithmetic mean is 12.
