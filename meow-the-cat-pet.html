18. meow, where's my milk, cried the cat is an example of?


Meow, where's my milk, cried the cat is an example of both onomatopoeia and personification. When we hear or read the word "meow", we instantly know it's about cats (onomatopoeia). Now when we say that the cat cried and asked, "where's my milk", it is making the cat human-like. Generally, cats or animals do not talk.(personification)Its onomatopeia and personification...
