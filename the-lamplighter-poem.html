1. who is lamplighter in the little prince ?


The Street Lamp LighterThe little prince is, at first, seduced by the street lamp lighter. His job is useful: he lights the lamps at sunset. But his planet turns more and more quickly, so he has to keep on switching his lamps off and on. "Those are the orders," says the lamplighter to the little prince, who, after all, respects the efforts of this grownup. "He is the only one who does not seem ridiculous to me. This may be because he deals with something else than himself."
