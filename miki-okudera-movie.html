17. miki was pulling a table with a force of 72 newton while kimi was pulling to the left of 89 newton what is the net force is exerted on the table?​


Net Force

[tex]\blue{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

Miki was pulling a table with a force of 72 newton while Kimi was pulling to the left of 89 newton what is the net force is exerted on the table?

Given:

F1 - 72 NF2 - 89 N

Solution & Answer:

[tex]\large\sf F_{net}=F_{1}+F_{2}[/tex]

Since there is a force exerted to the left direction, the force will be negative.

[tex]\large\sf F_{net}=72 \: N+-89 \: N[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf F_{net}=\boxed{\bold\red{17 \: N}}[/tex]

Hence, the net force is 17 N
