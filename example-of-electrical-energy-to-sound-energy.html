What is energy transformation?

One type of energy can change into another type of energy. Energy transformation means the changing of energy from one type to another, e.g. from kinetic energy to electrical energy, or from potential energy to kinetic energy.

What are some examples of energy transformation?

Here are some of the ways that energy can change (transform) from one type to another:

Sunshine

The Sun transforms nuclear energy into heat and light energy

food energy

Our bodies convert chemical energy in our food into mechanical energy for us to move

dog with fan

An electric fan transforms electrical energy into kinetic energy

lightning

Lightning converts electrical energy into light, heat and sound energy
