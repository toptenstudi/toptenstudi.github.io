19. 10. What is the estimate full opening of a sliding door?a. 45b. 90°C. 135°.


The estimate full opening of a sliding door is typically 90°.

A sliding door typically slides along a track and opens by moving to one side. When the door is fully open, it is perpendicular to the wall and forms an angle of 90° with the wall. This means that the door has opened to its maximum capacity and takes up the maximum amount of space in the doorway.

Option B, "90°," is the correct answer. Option A, "45," and Option C, "135°," are incorrect because they do not represent the full opening of the door.
