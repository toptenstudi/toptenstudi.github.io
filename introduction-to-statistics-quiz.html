4. Lily quiz scores are 73 , 75 , 89 , and 91 . What score in a fifth quiz will make her average quiz score at least 85?​


Answer:

97

Step-by-step explanation:

(x + 73 + 75 + 89 + 91) ÷ 5 = 85

(x + 328) ÷ 5 = 85

x + 328 = 425

x = 425 - 328

x ≥ 97
