23. Jennifer is j years old. Jennifer is 10 years younger than one-ninth Mary's age ( m). Answer: ?


AGE WORD PROBLEM

The answer to the problem is 9(j + 10). The problem involves an algebraic expressions to represent the relationship between unknown quantities. The problem states that  Jennifer is 10 years younger than one-ninth of Mary's age.

Given:

Jennifer's age: j yearsMary's age: m years

In the problem, Jennifer is 10 years younger than one-ninth of Mary's age. The problem is written like this:

j = (1/9)m - 10

To simplify the equation, We can determine Mary's age:(1/9)m = j + 10m = 9(j + 10)

The answer is    m = 9(j + 10).

The equation is j = (1/9)m - 10,  The equation represents the given information that Jennifer is 10 years younger than the one-ninth of Mary's age. By means simplifying the equation, we can isolate Mary's age and write it in terms of Jennifer's age as m = 9(j + 10).
