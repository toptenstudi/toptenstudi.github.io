USES OF INTERACTIVE MULTIMEDIA IN EDUCATION

Interactive multimedia tools promote active learning from children and are particularly essential in the areas that children struggle with, encouraging them to have a go at solving learning problems themselves and teaching other members of the class when they understand the lesson.

16. kahulugan ng interactive multimedia


Interactive multimedia, anumang elektronikong sistema na inihatid sa computer na nagpapahintulot sa gumagamit na kontrolin, pagsamahin, at manipulahin ang iba't ibang uri ng media, tulad ng teksto, tunog, video, graphics ng computer, at animation. Interactive multimedia isama ang computer, memory storage, digital (binary) data, telepono, telebisyon, at iba pang mga teknolohiya ng impormasyon. Kasama sa kanilang karaniwang mga application ang mga programa sa pagsasanay, mga video game, elektronikong ensiklopedya, at mga gabay sa paglalakbay. Multimedia and Interactivity

A multimedia is any information in a form of audio, video, animation, images or combination. Mostly of these are in digital form which means, you can access and see these in computers and related devices. These media or form of information can be transmitted and distributed by computers and related devices as well. This is the improved type of media wherein before there are just text or images.  

Interactivity means how computers would respond to user when he or she executes a keyboard or click command through mouse.  

Multimedia and interactivity are very much related to each other. In order to complete the user experience in games and using a website, these two should be combined. Thus, giving the user much unique and happier experience in accessing or giving out information.
