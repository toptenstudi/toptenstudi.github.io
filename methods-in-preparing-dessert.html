7. ways of preparing dessert


Finding inspiration and developing a great plated dessert is a chore in itself. But having to plan an entire dessert menu, to strike a balance of items to be offered, to choose items that will be attractive to your customers and promote good sales takes planning.

Answer: What are the important factors to be cosidered in plating and presenting desserts?

The following are the important factors to be considered in plating and presenting desserts. Food plating can affect the overall appearance of a dessert that helps to enhance the presentation.

1. The plate

2. Color

3. Texture

4. Keep things clean

5. Garnish to impress

THE PLATE

• The choice of plate is critical to the final presentation. The following are the tips to remember in choosing plate to use in plating.

1. Sizes

2. Shapes

3. Colors

Choosing right plate is vital because it is very important that the food should not be crowded.

COLOR

• Color is also vital in presentation of the dessert. Always try to have a variety of colors on the plate.

Green is fresh and cool, and can be soothing

Red is passion and excitement

Black is sophisticated and elegant

Blue is natural appetite suppressant, since it can make food look appetizing

TEXTURE

• Texture is critical food presentation, as well as enjoyment. Contrasting hard and soft, smooth and coarse, adds visual visual interest to food, and it will enhance your customers enjoyment to the food.

KEEP THINGS CLEAN

• Remember that neatness counts. Food should be contained within the rim of the plate, yet it should not be crowded in the center. Take a look at the plate and ask yourself if it is pleasing to the eye. It should not look sloppy and dirty.

GARNISH TO IMPRESS

• Garnishes and decorations can enhance your plate presentation. Choose garnishes that are appropriate to the ingredients. For Example, using a chocolate curls for chocolate cake

Finding inspiration and developing a great plated dessert is a chore in itself. But having to plan an entire dessert menu, to strike a balance of items to be offered, to choose items that will be attractive to your customers and promote good sales takes planning.

Establishments that have the most success with their dessert menu (and sales) tend to stick to the most popular flavours. Innovation just for innovation’s sake is generally not a good idea.
