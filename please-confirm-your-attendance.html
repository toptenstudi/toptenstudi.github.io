23. How can you make your actions be confirm with your faith? (Please help me I really don't know what to say...)​


Answer:

1. Receive the Sacraments. As Catholics, we are so blessed to be able to have guaranteed ways to receive grace. That is the gift of the sacraments. Attend Mass and go to confession. These acts not only bring grace, but tend to lighten any burdens you may be carrying.

2. Limit your TV time. Don’t watch TV that portrays the marriage relationship–or any relationship–in a negative light. When you stop to really think about it, most sitcoms, dramas, and reality TV show sin as normal and even preferable to living a holy life. Ask yourself: Does watching this TV show bring glory to God? If it doesn’t, consider giving it up.

ty

3. Pray for someone who has wronged you. And forgive them. It doesn’t really matter if they are sorry for what they did to you or if they even know they hurt you. God shows mercy to us every single minute of our lives, and it is our duty to pray for and forgive others.

4. Don’t gossip, and stand up for someone. When the conversation with your friends starts veering toward bashing others, sharing juicy details of someone else’s troubles, or anything of the sort, either stop the conversation by standing up for the person or just walk away. I know I have friends in my life who tend to gossip and not listen even when I try to defend someone, so sometimes the best option is to just walk away from the conversation.

5. Donate used clothes, toys, kitchen items, canned food, whatever to charity, and do so with a heart for Christ. We live in a world of constant surplus. I know I have a constant need to de-clutter, and “things” can take over if I don’t keep them in check. Our local Ladies of Charity and Salvation Army are always looking for donated items. Check to see if your church has a food pantry or a recommendation on where to donate. If you don’t have extra stuff to donate, maybe you can serve God by giving of your time and volunteering at church or at a charity

Do good deeds and things that pleases God.

Globalization is a process of international integration that occurs due to the exchange of world views, products, ideas, and other aspects of culture.

Explanation:

Globalization is the process of increasing interdependence between state and non-state actors on a global scale, so that social relations within a society are significantly shaped and influenced by the wider dimensions of social relations on a world scale. The process of globalization has actually been going on for a very long time, especially since the 15th century AD when Europeans made large-scale explorations by sailing across the ocean.

In today's era, the process of globalization seems to be unstoppable or even faster its spread. This is because technological advances in almost all fields support this. A small example of globalization is the connection of the internet network that can connect people from two different cities to two different continents. In short, the process of globalization is still ongoing. All cultural values ​​from other nations can be easily identified with internet services. With globalization, the world is so vast and the distances between countries are no longer a barrier to interact with each other. The impact of globalization is also very large. The process of globalization is supported by advances in information technology, communication, and transportation. With these advances, human relations have become easier.

Characteristics of Globalization:

Boundaries between countries are getting thinner In this world there are more than two hundred countries. Each country has a specific territory and state boundaries. Due to the influence of globalization, boundaries between countries are no longer important. Information is easy to spread. The process of globalization is the dissemination of information becomes easier and faster. Trading activities are increasing. growing international trade. Trade activities continue to grow due to the influence of globalization in the economic field. Economic activities and trade in the world are becoming increasingly open across national boundaries. Not only that, current economic activity is also related to the problem of labor migration. In the global era, workers can choose to work in any country according to their wishes and abilities.
