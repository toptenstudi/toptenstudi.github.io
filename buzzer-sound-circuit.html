B. If the fan is removed from the circuit, it is likely that the circuit will still be complete and the buzzer and bulb will continue to function. This is because the fan is likely in parallel with the other components, so the current does not need to flow through the fan for the circuit to be complete. However, the overall resistance of the circuit will be lower without the fan, which may affect the performance of the other components.
