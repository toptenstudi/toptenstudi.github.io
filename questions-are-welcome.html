24. 1. the students welcome the new teacher. 2. the new teachers is welcomed by the students. question; what are the changes made in the sentences construction from active to passive voice?


Sentence 1: The students welcome the new teacher.

Sentence 2: The new teachers are welcomed by the students.

The changes made in the sentences construction from active to passive voice are the subject and the object (without changing the actual meaning).

FURTHER EXPLANATION:

Passive sentences are sentences that are subject to an action or activity, an implementation of the subject. While Active sentences are sentences where the subject becomes the doer of an activity. An Active sentence can be changed into a Passive sentence without changing the meaning. But what has changed is the subject.

Sentences in English are grouped into active and passive sentences. Active voice is a sentence where the subject becomes the doer of an activity. If in active sentences the subject is an active subject, then in passive sentences the subject is a passive subject. Likewise to change passive sentences to active sentences.

We should pay attention to the subject first. then we change the subject of the passive sentence in the form of a passive subject, we change the verb to be an active verb.

Here is the Formula:

Passive sentence: Passive Subject + Passive Verb + ObjectActive voice: Active Subject + Active Verb + Object
