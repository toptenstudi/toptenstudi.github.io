Since we need a 3 digit code, we can choose the first digit in 5 ways, the second digit in 5 ways, and the third digit in 5 ways. Try substituting any number from 0 to 9 for A (the middle digit or tens place) , then divide by 11 members.

Option 1:  A = 2  ⇒ $1 2 2
$122 ÷ 11 = $11.09090909...

Option 2:  A = 3  ⇒  $1 3 2
$ 132 ÷ 11 = $12

Option 3:  A = 9   ⇒     $ 1 9 2
$ 192 ÷ 11 = $ 17,45454545,,,

When you substitute the rest of the one-digit number for A, the decimals are repeating.  (Amazing, right? Try it)
