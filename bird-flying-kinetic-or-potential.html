8. A 0.10 kilogram bird is flying at a constant speed of 8.0 m/s. What is the bird'skinetic energy?​


Remember the formula: KE = ½mv²

Your givens are:

m = 0.10 kg

v = 8.0 m/s

Substitute the values then solve for the kinetic energy KE:

KE = ½mv²

KE = ½ (0.10 kg) (8.0 m/s)²

KE = 3.2 J

KE= 1/2(0.50 kg)(7.0 m/s)²
