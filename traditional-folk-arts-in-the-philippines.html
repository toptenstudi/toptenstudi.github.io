1. Enumerate 5 traditional folk arts in the Philippines


There are a lot of Traditional Folk arts in the Philippines and here are some of them

1. Wooden Bangca – wooden bangca also known as Bangka, Baroto, pump Boars etc., are the primary transportation in the Philippines. Filipinos commonly use it for their traiding, fishing, traveling and for their transport.

2. Pina fabric – It is a fiber made from pineapple leaves that are common in the Philippines. The major use of this fabric is for the creation of barong tagalog and other formal wear in the Philippines.  

3. Bahay kubo/Nipa Hut – nipa huts are native houses of the indigenous people of the Philippines. It is made from plant materials like bamboo and driend leaves. It serves as an icon of Philippine culture.  

4. Carving – Paete Laguna is the woodcarving capital of the Philippines. Wood is a common material used in carving because of its durability and it is easier to carve.

5. Literature – The story of “Malakas at Maganda” is a good example of Philippine literature because of its Moral lesson that Filipinos admire.
