[tex]\underline{\mathbb{QUESTION:}}[/tex]

- What is the next two terms in the sequence 6, 10, 14, 18,...?

A. 22, 26 B. 26, 22 C. 23, 27 D. 27, 23

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{ANSWER:}}[/tex]

[tex]\qquad\LARGE\rm» \:\: \green{A.\:22\:and\:26}[/tex]

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

- Determine the common difference.

[tex]\begin{aligned}&\bold{\color{lightblue}Formula:}\\&\boxed{d=a_n-a_{n-1}}\end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]d = a_2 - a_1 = 10 - 6 = 4[/tex]

[tex]d = a_3- a_2 = 14 - 10 = 4[/tex]

[tex]d = a_4- a_3 = 18 - 14 = 4[/tex]

- Thus, the common difference is 4, find the next two terms.

[tex]a_5 = a_4 + d = 18 + 4 = 22[/tex]

[tex]a_6 = a_5 + d = 22 + 4 = 26[/tex]

[tex]\therefore[/tex] The next two terms in the sequence are 22 and 26.
