2. how to mentain and sanitize the nail cutter, nipper, and pusher.​


Answer:

Maintaining and sanitizing your nail cutter, nipper, and pusher is essential to prevent the spread of infections and maintain healthy nails. Here are some steps to help you properly sanitize and maintain your tools:

Clean: First, clean your nail cutter, nipper, and pusher using soap and water. Use a soft-bristled brush to scrub off any dirt or debris. Be sure to dry them thoroughly before moving on to the next step.

Sanitize: Next, sanitize your tools using either rubbing alcohol or hydrogen peroxide. You can use a cotton pad or swab to apply the sanitizer to the blades and other parts of the tool. Let it sit for a few minutes before wiping it off with a clean cloth.

Sterilize: To sterilize your nail cutter, nipper, and pusher, you can use a sterilizing solution such as barbicide or autoclave. Follow the instructions on the product label and submerge the tools in the solution for the recommended amount of time.

Store: Once you have sanitized and sterilized your tools, store them in a clean and dry place. Avoid storing them in a damp or humid environment, as this can promote the growth of bacteria and fungi.

It is important to note that these steps should be done after each use, especially if you are using your nail cutter, nipper, and pusher on multiple people. By following these simple steps, you can ensure that your tools are properly sanitized and maintained, and help prevent the spread of infections.
