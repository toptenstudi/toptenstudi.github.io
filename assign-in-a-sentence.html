8. Assignment:Active Voice (definition and sample sentences)Passive voice (definition and sample sentences)​


Answer:

Active Voice

Definition: Active voice means that a sentence has a subject that acts upon its verb.

Sample sentences:

Manny ate six eggs at dinner.

Danny replaced the flat tire.

Romy painted the entire house.

Passive Voice

Definition: Passive voice means that a subject is a recipient of a verb’s action.

Sample sentences:

At breakfast, six cupcakes were eaten by Manny.

The flat tire was replaced by Danny.

The entire house was painted by Romy.

Explanation:

Hope this help!

Active voice means that a sentence has a subject that acts upon its verb.

Examples:
The teacher answers the student's question.

Ana called David on the phone.


Pattern: subject+verb+object


Passive voice means that a subject is a recipient of a verb’s action.

Examples:
The teacher's question is answered by the students.

David was called by Ana on the phone.


Pattern:subject(receiver of the action)+be+past participle+by+doer of the action.
