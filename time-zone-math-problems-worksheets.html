7 days make up one week.15 days make up a fortnight.Between 28 and 31 days make up one month.3 months make up a quarter.6 months make up a semester.12 months form one year.2 years form a biennium.10 years form a decade.100 years form a century.1000 years make up a millennium.

Answer:

Heres your answer!

Step-by-step explanation:

Assuming that there are no Daylight Saving Time changes happening on the specific dates, here are the local times for each time zone based on 10:00 a.m. in Manila (UTC+8):

1. Hong Kong Time Zone: UTC+8  (same as Manila time) , Local Time: 10:00 AM

2. Bangkok Time Zone: UTC+7 (one hour behind Manila time), Local Time: 9:00 AM

3. New Orleans Time Zone (Central Daylight Time): UTC-5 (13 hours behind Manila time), Local Time: 9:00 PM (Previous Day)

4. Cape Verde Time Zone: UTC-1 (9 hours behind Manila time), Local Time: 1:00 AM (Next Day)

5. Brisbane Time Zone: UTC+10 (2 hours ahead of Manila time), Local Time: 12:00 PM (noon)
