⇒  The number of ways in which we can get exactly  3  heads when a fair coin is tossed  4  times is  C(4,3)=4.  

The total number of outcomes when a fair coin is tossed  n  times is  2n .

⇒  The total number of outcomes when a fair coin is tossed  4  times is  24=16 .

The probability of getting exactly  r  heads when a fair coin is tossed r times  =C(n,r)2n.  

⇒  The probability of getting exactly  3  heads when a fair coin is tossed  4  times  =416=14.
