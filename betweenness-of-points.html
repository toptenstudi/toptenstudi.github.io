28. determine the energy expended moving a charge of 100 mC between two points if the voltage between the point is 3 V.


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The formula for the energy expended by moving a charge is

[tex]\boxed{W = QV}[/tex]

where:

W = energy

Q = charge

V = voltage

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Listing the given values

Q = 100 mC = 100 × 10⁻³ C

V = 3 V

Solving for W

[tex]W = QV[/tex]

[tex]W = (100 \times 10^{-3} \: \text{C})(\text{3 V})[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{W = \text{0.3 J}}[/tex]

Therefore, the energy expended is 0.3 J.
