What is the difference of these two temperatures? 2 Kathlyn deposited P2 000 in a bank. After two months, she withdrew P500 How much money is left in her bank account? 3. Clarisse had earned of P5 000 last week. But this week, she lost of P1 000. Find the difference between the two amounts.​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Difference = Higher temperature - Lower temperature

  Difference = 10°F - 4°F

  Difference = 6°F

2. Amount left = Amount deposited - Amount withdrawn

   Amount left = P2,000 - P500

   Amount left = P1,500

3. Difference = Amount earned - Amount lost

   Difference = P5,000 - P1,000

   Difference = P4,000

Answer:

The difference between the morning temperature and the evening temperature is 6°F. This is calculated by subtracting the evening temperature (4°F) from the morning temperature (10°F): 10°F - 4°F = 6°F.

After Kathlyn withdrew P500 from her account, she is left with P1 500. This is calculated by subtracting the amount she withdrew (P500) from her initial deposit (P2 000): P2 000 - P500 = P1 500.

Clarisse's earnings last week was P5 000, and she lost P1 000 this week. To find the difference between the two amounts, we need to subtract the amount she lost (P1 000) from her earnings last week (P5 000): P5 000 - P1 000 = P4 000. Therefore, the difference between the two amounts is P4 000.
