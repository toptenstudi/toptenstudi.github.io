13. administration and administrative theres have differences?​


Answer:

Yes, there is a difference between "administration" and "administrative".

"Administration" refers to the management and organization of an institution or system. It involves the planning, organizing, coordinating, and controlling of resources and processes to achieve the goals of the institution or system.

"Administrative", on the other hand, refers to the tasks or duties that are involved in the management and organization of an institution or system. It refers to the day-to-day operations of an organization or system, including tasks such as record-keeping, scheduling, and communication.

In summary, "administration" refers to the overall management and organization of an institution or system, while "administrative" refers to the specific tasks or duties involved in that management and organization.
