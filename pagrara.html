EXPLANATION:Hablon is Hiligaynon for “something woven,” from the root word habol, “to weave”. It refers to the hand-woven textiles by Kiniray-a and Hiligaynon weavers. In a Panayanon legend, ten datus from Borneo landed on Panay Island, established settlements and ushered in an era of development.
