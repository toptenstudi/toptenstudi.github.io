Geometric sequence is a topic in math that students learn at elementary schools. However, we also learn it in junior high or even high school, while of course the problems and the numbers are getting more complicated. An example of geometric sequence is 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64...

Personally, this is rather fun of a topic to learn, since it deals with a fixed pattern that we can easily spot if we take on more and more exercises and questions on this topic.
