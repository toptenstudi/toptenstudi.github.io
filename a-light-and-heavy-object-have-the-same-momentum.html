Determine which has more momentum, the heavy truck that is moving at 30 km/h or the light truck that is moving at 30 km/h?  

 

The heavier truck has more momentum than the lighter one, so the answer is the letter (a).

Explanation:

Because momentum is influenced by the mass and speed of the moving object, it is clear that although the two trucks in the problem are moving at an identical speed which is both 30 km/h, the weight of the heavy truck will increase the momentum once it is multiplied to that amount.  

So any change in both speed and weight will affect the level of the momentum of a moving object.
