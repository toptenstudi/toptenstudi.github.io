3. the count of Monte Cristo was Rizal's favorite novel. what characteristics do edmond dantes ( lead character) have which can also be seen in Dr. jose Rizal and how did this help Rizal pursue his life time mission​


Answer:

Jose Rizal read and admired the novel "The Count of Monte-Cristo" by French author Alexander Dumas. Quite often people who read Rizal and this work of Dumas, relay the character of Edmond Dantes with that of Simoun (El Filibusterismo). The plot of revenge and the lonely figure of an avenging hero is too similar to be ignored. The difference came in the ending of the two novels. While Edmond Dantes found fulfillment in his life, Rizal gave a tragic end for Simoun (Ibarra).
