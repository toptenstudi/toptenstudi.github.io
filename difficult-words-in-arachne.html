25. What does arachne do that makes it difficult for her to solve her problem?


Answer:

What does Arachne do that makes it hard for her to solve her problem?

What Arachne does and does it very well is weaving beautiful embroideries. Controlling her pride prevented her from being humble, and that made it difficult for her to solve her problem.

Explanation:

The skill she possesses is both a gift and a curse after reading her story. The very thing that brought her fame and probably fortune by looking at the number of people who wanted to buy her works also drove her towards a conflict with the Goddess Athena.  

Because of her unmatched skill in embroidery, she did the following:

She became famous throughout the land. News of her skill reached Olympus. She became too proud of it to the point of arrogance. Her misguided confidence even made her humiliate nearly everyone in Olympus and the underworld. She agreed to Athena’s challenge, which is a big mistake.

How her work further angered the gods which in turn led to her problem:

She continually insisted that she was better than Athena. She said her skills were not from the goddess but is hers alone. Images of heavenly wickedness, degeneracy and depravity were created by her on her embroidered work.

Arachne’s problem is her pride which she cannot control for some reason. Any thinking individual would know better than to accept a challenge from a god or goddess who has the ability to turn you into anything. The narcissistic nature of the Greek Gods would surely bring trouble for anyone that crosses them. In the end, Arachne became the first spider, a form that allows her to continue spinning her thread to this day.
