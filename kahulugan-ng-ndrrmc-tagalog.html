13. Ano ginagawa Ng NDRRMC?​


The NDRRMC plans and leads the guiding activities in the field of communication, warning signals, emergency, transportation, evacuation, rescue, engineering, health and rehabilitation, public education and auxiliary services such as fire fighting and the police in the country.

18. Ano Ang Kahulugan Ng PAGASA- PHIVOLCS- NDRRMC


Answer:

PAGASA- Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration.

the National Meteorological and Hydrological Services agency of the Philippines mandated to provide protection.

PHIVOLCS- Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology.

is a Philippine national institution dedicated to provide information on the activities of volcanoes, earthquakes, and tsunamis.

NDRRMC- National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council.

formerly known as the National Disaster Coordinating Council, is a working group of various government, non-government, civil sector.


19. TUNGKULIN NG NDRRMC


Answer:

Ito ay sangay ng gobyerno na kung saan nagbibigay babala, impormasyon, abiso o mga mahahalagang anunsiyo tuwing may sakuna o kalamidad sa pilipinas. Responsibilidad ng National Disaster Risk Reduction Management Council NDRRMC ang pagsasama-sama, koordinasyon at pangangasiwa sa pagbuo at pagpapatupad ng mga ahensiya at organisasyon sa mga batas, programa, plano, mga alituntunin at teknikal na pamantayan ng RA 10121.
