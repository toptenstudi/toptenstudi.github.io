y - y₁ = [tex] \frac{ y_{2}- y_{1} }{ x_{2} - x_{1} } [/tex] (x - x₁)

Example:

Find the equation of the line in Standard and General Forms passing through points (4,2) and (1, 5).

Solution:

y - 2 = ---------- (x - 4)
              1 - 4

y = -x + 4 + 2
