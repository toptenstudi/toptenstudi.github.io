30. Mia hired a moving company. The company charged $500 for its services, and Mia gives the movers a 16% tip. How much does Mia tip the movers?


Answer:

$580


Step-by-step explanation:

Moving Charge = $500


Tips = 16% of $500

Tips = 0.16 x 500 = $80


Total charges = 500 + 80 = $580


Answer: Mia has to pay $580
