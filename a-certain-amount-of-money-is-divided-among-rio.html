7. A certain amount of money is divided among Rio, Kim and Leo in the ratio 5:7:3. If Leo gets PHP 24,000.00, how much is the total amount? ​


Step-by-step explanation:

Let:

x = the proportionality amount between three of them.

t = total amount.

So we have a general equation as follows:

[tex]5x + 7x + 3x = t[/tex]

Note that:

5x = amount Rio gets

7x = amount Kim gets

3x = amoun Leo gets

We know that Leo gets 24,000 pesos, therefore we have:

[tex]3x = 24000 \\ x = 8000 \: pesos[/tex]

Substituting this to our general equation.

[tex]5(8000) + 7(8000) + 24000 = t \\ t = 120000 \: pesos[/tex]

13. A certain amount of money is divided among Rio, Kim and Leo in the ratio 5:7:3. If Leo gets ₱24,000.00,how much is the total amount

Total portions = 5+7+3 = 15

Leo gets 3 portions of the total amount equal to Php 24,000.  So each portion is equal to (Php 24,000 / 3) or Php 8,000.

Therefore, the Total Amount = Amount per portion x Total portions
Total Amount = Php8,000 x 15

Total Amount =  Php 120,000
