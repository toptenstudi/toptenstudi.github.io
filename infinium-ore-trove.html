10. what is an ore deposit?​


Answer:

An ore deposit is a natural concentration of one or more minerals within the host rock. It has a definite shape on economic criteria with finite quantity (tons) and average quality (grade). The shape varies according to the complex nature of the deposit such as layered, disseminated, veins, folded, and deformed.

25. How long will it take tRove to cover the 3 km distance from her school to her house with a walking speed of 6 km/h?​


Answer:

The answer is the explanation formula

Explanation:

We can use the formula:

time = distance / speed

where distance is the distance between the school and the house, and speed is the walking speed of Tove.

Plugging in the values we have:

time = 3 km / 6 km/h

time = 0.5 h

Therefore, it will take Tove 0.5 hours (30 minutes) to cover the 3 km distance from her school to her house with a walking speed of 6 km/h.
