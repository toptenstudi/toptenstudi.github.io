16. What are the different kinds of sewing machine? Differentiate each according toits function.​


Answer:

MECHANICAL SEWING MACHINE-A mechanical machine is more or less a “basic machine” that uses knobs to adjust the settings. Those that are higher end may have more decorative stitches and buttonhole stitches than those on the lower end.

ELECTRONIC SEWING MACHINE-Coming into its features, electronic sewing machines are useful for - good quality of stitching, minimum of 7 in-built stitch capability, embroidering, hemming, blind stitch, buttonhole stitch, , hemming zip fixing, smocking, reverse - stitch lever, stretch stitching, quilting, built in needle Threader, top loading.

COMPUTERIZED OR AUTOMATED SEWING MACHINE-Computerized sewing machines feature small monitor displays for easier operation. A computer is used to control several different motors, giving you the ability to precisely move the needle bar, the tensioning discs, the feed dog, and other sewing machine parts.

EMBROIDERY MACHINE-Machine embroidery is an embroidery process whereby a sewing machine or embroidery machine is used to create patterns on textiles. It is used commercially in product branding, corporate advertising, and uniform adornment. It is also used in the fashion industry to decorate garments and apparel.

OVERLOCK SEWING MACHINE-An overlock sewing machine, or serger as it is commonly known, can trim, stitch, and overcast seams as they sew. They can also be used for applications such as inserting a zipper and creating beautiful decorations with certain stitches. ... The overlock can also be used to finish the edges of seams.
