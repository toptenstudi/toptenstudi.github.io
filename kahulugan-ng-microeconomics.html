Ang kahulugan ng microeconomics ay ito: ang microeconomics ay ang sangay na naglalaan ng pokus sa pagpili at pag-uugali ng mga konsyumer, prodyuser at pamilihan. Ito ay mas tiyak at tumutukoy sa maliit na yunit ng kabuhayan, kumpara sa isa pang sangay ng ekonomiks na makroekomics (macroeconomics).

15. ano ang pinagkaiba ng microeconomics sa macroeconomics?


Macroeconomics VS Microeconomics Economics is separated into two classifications: Microeconomics is the study a person and the decision of a business.Macroeconomics looks at the decisions of the whole country and its government.

While these two divisions of economics look to be different, they are essentially symbiotic and match one another since there are many issues overlapping between the two fields.

Microeconomics – Focuses on the supply and demand that determine the price level in the economy. Microeconomics tries to understand what choices people makes and allocation of resources.

Example: Microeconomics examines how a business could maximize its capacity and production so that it could better compete in its field.

Several key principles in microeconomics: Supply,Demand, and Balance -  when supply equals demand the economy will have an equilibrium or a balanced economyProduction Theory – the study about production Costs of production – the price of goods is determined by the cost of the resources used during the production period. Labor economics – It looks at the pattern of wages, employment, and income of workers and employers.

Macroeconomics

Macroeconomics – It studies the behavior of a country and how its rules affect its economy as a whole. It analyzes the entire business industries rather than focusing on individual business.

Example:  Macroeconomics examines how a country could maximize its GDP

25. Ipaliwanah ang pinakaiba ng microeconomics at macroeconomics


Pagkakaiba ng Makroekonomiks at Maykroekonomiks

Ang pagkakaiba ng macroekonomics at maykroeconomics ay matutukoy batay sa mga saklaw nito. Pareho silang sangay ng ekonomiks ngunit ang pagkakaiba ay ang tinututukan o pinag-aaralan sa galaw ng ekonomiya.

Ang makroekonomiks (macroeconomics) ay sangay ng ekonomics na sumasaklaw tungkol sa pag-aaral sa galaw ng ekonomiya bilang isang kabuuan (as a whole economy) habang ang maykroekonomiks (microeconomics) ay sangay ng ekonomiks na sumasaklaw tungkol sa pag-aaral sa galaw ng ekonomiya batay sa bawat indibidwal, maliit na yunit ng lipunan o industriya.

Ang makroekonomiks ay tunkol sa mga ekonomiks na usapin tulad ng implasyon, paglaki ng kita sa nasyonal, rehiyonal o global na saklaw habang ang maykroekonomiks ay tunkol sa mga ekonomiks na usapin tulad ng paglaki ng kita ng isang industriya, indibidwal o maliit na yunit ng lipunan at surplus ng prodyuser at konsumer.
