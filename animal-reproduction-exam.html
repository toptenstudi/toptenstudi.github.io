1. I'm the Map!Reproduction in AnimalsSexual ReproductionAserual ReproductionExamExampleExampleExampleection: Complete the concept map by describing sexual and asexual reproduction​


Answer:

Asexual reproduction involves one parent and produces offspring that are genetically identical to each other and to the parent.

Sexual reproduction involves two parents and produces offspring that are genetically unique.

During sexual reproduction, two haploid gametes join in the process of fertilization to produce a diploid zygote.

Meiosis is the type of cell division that produces gametes.

12. Suppose your grades on three exams are 80,93, and 91, what grade will you need on your next exam to have an average of 90 on the four exams?


Hello, reapthereaper2322! I will be answering your question today...

---------------------------------------------------------

Your question is:

Suppose your grades on three exams are 80, 93, and 91. What grade will you need on your next exam to have an average of 90 on the four exams?

We will use the formula for calculating the mean average to solve for x where x is the required grade on the next exam to have an average of 90 on all the four exams.

[tex] \large \text{Average} = \frac{\text{sum of the values}}{\text{number of the values}} [/tex]

[tex] 90 = \frac{ 80 + 93 + 91 + x }{4} [/tex]

[tex] 4( 90 = \frac{ 80 + 93 + 91 + x }{4}) 4 [/tex]

[tex] 360 = 80 + 93 + 91 + x [/tex]

[tex] 360 - 80 - 93 - 91 = x [/tex]

[tex] \large \green{x = 96} [/tex]

Therefore, you will need a grade of at least 96 on your last exam to have an average of at least 90 on all the four exams.
