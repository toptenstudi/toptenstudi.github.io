= 1 - ( 1/3 + 3/6)
= 1 - (2/6 + 3/6)
= 1 - 5/6
= 6/6 - 5/6
= 1/6

28. A farmer own a hectare of land.he gave 1/4 to his son and 3/5 to his daughter how much land was left?


Let 'x' be the amount of land left
As the entire land represents 1 quantity(hectare) then we have the expression as:
x = 1-(1/4+3/5)
[tex]x = 1 - ( \frac{1}{4} + \frac{3}{5} ) \\ x = 1 - ( \frac{5 + 12}{20} ) \\ x = 1 - \frac{17}{20} \\ x = \frac{20}{20} - \frac{17}{20} \\ x = \frac{3}{20} [/tex]
