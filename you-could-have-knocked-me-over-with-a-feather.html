17. you could have knocked me over with a feather​


"You could have knocked me over with a feather" is a common idiom used to express surprise or shock at something unexpected or surprising. It means that the speaker is so surprised that they feel as if they could be knocked over by a feather, which is a very light object. It is often used to emphasize the degree of surprise, shock, or disbelief that a person is experiencing.
