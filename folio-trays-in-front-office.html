17. What front office document contains the personal and financial information of guests that would help in the development of the hotel’s marketing strategies? Select one: a. Folio Tray b. Guest History Record c. Account Posting Document d. Feedback Form


Answer:

b. Guest History Record

The Guest History Record is a front office document that contains the personal and financial information of guests, such as their name, address, email, phone number, payment method, length of stay, and room preferences. This document is essential for developing the hotel's marketing strategies because it provides insights into the guest's behavior, needs, and preferences. By analyzing the guest history record, hotels can identify trends, patterns, and opportunities to create targeted marketing campaigns and personalized experiences that enhance the guest's satisfaction and loyalty. The folio tray is a storage unit for guest folios, the account posting document records financial transactions for the guest, and feedback forms collect guest feedback and suggestions.
