4. 4. As rupture along a fault starts, how do you describe the path of energy waves travelling outward from the focus? A curve B. linear fashion C. straight-line D. spherical fashion​


Answer:

D. SPHERICAL FASHION

Explanation:

As rupture along a fault initiates, waves of energy travel outward from the hypocenter in a spherical fashion. These waves of energy are strongest nearest the hypocenter but gradually grow weaker further away from the site of initial rupture.
