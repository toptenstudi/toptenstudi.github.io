2. Mary is 24 yrs old. Mary is twice as old as merly was, when mary was as cold as merly is now. How old is merly now?


Question:

Mary is 24 yrs old. Mary is twice as old as Merly was when Mary was as old as Merly is now. How old is Merly now?


Answer:

16 years old


Step-by-step explanation:

Let Merly be x now


When Mary was x, Mary was twice as old as Merly

⇒ Merly was 1/2x then


Find the difference in age:

Difference = x - 1/2x = 1/2x


Information we know:

Mary is 24 years old now

Mery is x years old now

Merly is 1/2 x younger than Mary


Solve x:

24 - x = 1/2x

3/2 x = 24

x = 24 ÷ 3/2

x = 16 years old


Answer: Merly is 16 years old now.

25. How Old is the Sun? and. how old is the Earth


Answer:

The Sun age is 4.306 billion years old, while the Earth age is 4.543 billion years old

Question

How Old is the Sun?

How Old is the Earth?

Answer

Sun Age - 4.603 billion years

Earth Age-4.543 Billion Years Olds

Explanation

°Sun----The sun formed more than 4.5 billion years ago, when a cloud of dust and gas called a nebula collapsed under its own gravity. As it did, the cloud spun and flattened into a disk, with our sun forming at its center.

°Earth----Today, we know from radiometric dating that Earth is about 4.5 billion years old. Had naturalists in the 1700s and 1800s known Earth's true age, early ideas about evolution might have been taken more seriously.
