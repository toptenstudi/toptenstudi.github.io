Online courses that simulate the classroom online is called Courseware.

Definition of Courseware

Courseware refers to software programs specifically designed for educational purposes. Originally available on CD as a course aid to instructors and to help them manage lectures, courseware has been revolutionized. Most are now available online with a variety of resources and supporting materials (reference books, research papers and journals).

Courseware is primarily used for online courses and certifications such as Microsoft Certified Professional courses.

Coverage in courseware

Courseware educational material studied with the help of a computer. It can be in the form of flash drives, compact disks or material placed online. Courseware can include:

Instructions on how to divide courses into lectures Notes to the instructor on leading a class Information for independent and computer-based learning Useful Web URLs for related materials
