Actually there are six forces in the macro environmental opportunities, they are:

1. economic forces

2. technological forces

3. demographic forces

4. natural or ecological forces

5. political and legal forces

6. socio cultural forces

Entrepreneurs must pay attention to the entities in order to seek for opportunities to their businesses. All the entities have its own influences. Economic forces influence the production level and the decision-making process. entrepreneurs must upgrade their skill in technology for the production such as the perform of machinery, securities, internet speed for its marketing. Every business must pay attention to the natural forces as it affects the sustainability of the business itself such as the entrepreneurs must pay attention to the climate change, pollution or regulation of environmental life. Risk Tolerance/Risk taking

Entrepreneurship is often associated with risk. While it’s true that launching a venture requires an entrepreneur to take risks, they also need to take steps to minimize it.

Innovation

Innovation is a characteristic some, but not all, entrepreneurs possess. Fortunately, it’s a type of strategic mindset that can be cultivated. By developing your strategic thinking skills, you can be well-equipped to spot innovative opportunities and position your venture for success.

Creativity

Creativity gives birth to something new. For without creativity, there is no innovation possible. Entrepreneurs usually have the knack to pin down a lot of ideas and act on them. Not necessarily every idea might be a hit. But the experience obtained is gold.

Professionalism

Professionalism is a quality which all good entrepreneurs must possess. An entrepreneurs mannerisms and behavior with their employees and clientele goes a long way in developing the culture of the organization.

Optimism

When you’re just starting out, it can seem like getting your business off the ground will never happen. But entrepreneurs don’t think like that. They are optimistic about the future and are always looking ahead.
