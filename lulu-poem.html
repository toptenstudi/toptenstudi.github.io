2. When Zoe was 6 years old, Lulu was twice her age. Zoe is 10 years old now, how old is Lulu?​


Answer:

Lulu is now 20.

Step-by-step explanation:

Zoe's age will be times 2 because it says Lulu was twice her age. Zoe is ten years old now but we are looking for Lulu's age; 10x2=20.

Answer:

16 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

When Zoe was 6 years old, Lulu was twice her age:

⇒ Lulu was 12 years old then

⇒ Difference in their age = 12 - 6 = 6 years

⇒ Lulu was 6 years older than Zoe

When Zoe is 10 years old,

⇒ Lulu will continue to be 6 years older than Zoe

⇒ Lulu will be 10 + 6 = 16 years old.

Answer: Lulu is 16 years old now.
