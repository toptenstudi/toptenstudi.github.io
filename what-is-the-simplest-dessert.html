8. Which of the following is considered the simplest dessert? a. Custard b. Fruits c. Gelatin d. Puddings


Answer:

From the choices, fruits, letter C is considered as the simplest dessert.

Explanation:

Let us define the word simple and dessert first so that we can identify which choice is the most appropriate from all the choices.

Simple is something that is plain, uncomplicated, and/or in basic form. Dessert is a type of food that is sweet and usually served after the main course.

The choices are all types of dessert but we are looking for the dessert that belongs to the simple dessert category. Basically, simple dessert is a dessert that is prepared with less or minimum ingredients and preparation time. Or it can be a dessert that needs no preparation at all; the main ingredient is already good to go.

Analyzing the choices, only one choice stands out; fruits. Fruit is served as it is. Peeling or slicing is the only preparation you'll have to do. Unlike the other choices, you'll need other ingredients and some preparation steps before you can serve it. Hence, fruits are considered the simplest dessert among the choices.
