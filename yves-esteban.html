24. Max, Grace, and Yve are given a total of 210 as a gift from their uncle. They share it in the ratio of their age. If Max is the eldest and he is 10 years older than Yve. Yve will be turning 11 years old this year and Grace is 2 years older than her. How much does each receive?show solution please​


Answer:

Max receives $100/7, Grace receives $11, and Yve receives $9.

Solution:

Let X be the amount of money Max receives, Y be the amount of money Grace receives, and Z be the amount of money Yve receives. We can set up a system of equations based on the given information:

X + Y + Z = 210 (total amount of money they receive)X : Y : Z = 21 : 20 : 10 (ratio of their ages)X = Z + 10 (Max is 10 years older than Yve)Y = Z + 2 (Grace is 2 years older than Yve)

To solve for the unknowns X, Y, and Z, we can use substitution. From equation 3, we can substitute X with Z+10 in equation 2, giving:

(Z+10) : Y : Z = 21 : 20 : 10

We can simplify this by multiplying all terms by 10:

210 + 10Y = 20Z + 200

10Y = 20Z - 10

Similarly, from equation 4, we can substitute Y with Z+2 in equation 2, giving:

X : (Z+2) : Z = 21 : 20 : 10

We can simplify this by multiplying all terms by 10:

21X = 20Z + 200

X = (20Z + 200)/21

Now, we can substitute the expression for X in the first equation to get:

(20Z + 200)/21 + (Z+2) + Z = 210

Simplifying this equation gives:

42Z = 378

Z = 9

Using this value of Z, we can find X and Y by substituting in the equations for X and Y that we derived earlier:

X = (20Z + 200)/21 = 100/7

Y = Z + 2 = 11

Therefore, Max receives $100/7, Grace receives $11, and Yve receives $9.
