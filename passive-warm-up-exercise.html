3. which of the following is an example of a passive warm-up exercise


Answer:

Passive warm ups increase temperature by external means. Such methods include massages, heating pads, steam baths or hot showers. Passive warm up involves raising muscle or core temperature by some external means, while active warm up utilises exercise. Passive heating allows one to obtain the increase in muscle or core temperature achieved by active warm up without depleting energy substrates.
