Answer:

1. A recent study found that students who watch TV during the week do not do as  well in school.

- reinforce and emphasize the author’s viewpoint

Anaphora works as a literary device to allow writers to convey, emphasize, and reinforce meaning. This open a statement to back up a result or consequence of one’s behavior.

- make the author seem knowledgeable, reliable and correct
