This is due to the potential energy which was stored in the spring which will be later on converted into a kinetic energy.

To explain this:

When a toy is wound up, a work is done causing the spring to be fixed tightly. This work is stored in the form of a potential energy.

Once it is released, the potential energy will be released as a kinetic energy causing the wheels of the toy to move.
