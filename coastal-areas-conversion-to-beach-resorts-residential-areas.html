10. describe the three coastal processes that affect the coastal areas​


Answer:

These are corrasion, abrasion, hydraulic action, attrition and corrosion/solution. Corrasion is when waves pick up beach material (e.g. pebbles) and hurl them at the base of a cliff. Abrasion occurs as breaking waves which contain sand and larger fragments erode the shoreline or headland.

19. Which is cooler coastal areas or inland areas?​


Answer:

Which is cooler coastal areas or inland areas?​

Costal areas are cooler than inland areas.

Why are coastal areas cooler than inland areas?

Water has a higher heat capacity than soil and rock, so the ocean takes much longer to heat and to cool than the land. Coastal areas will generally have more moderate temperatures than inland areas because of the heat capacity of the ocean.
