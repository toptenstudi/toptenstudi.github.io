COVID-19 PANDEMIC

Claim of Fact:

CoronaVirus is harmful to humans. It can be transmitted human to human.

Claim of Policy:

We should wear our face mask and face shield everytime we go out. Observe the social distancing.

Claim of Value:

It's better if we stay at our homes than to go out because you will be safe in CoVid-19.

If kung hindi po kayo satisfied sa answers ko, pwede po kayong gumawa ng inyo. Para mas madali bibigyan ko po kayo ng meaning at examples ng Claim of Fact, Policy and Value.

Claim of value argues that something is good or bad, or that one thing is better than another thing. Sample claims of value: It's better to apply good nutritional choices at home than teach them at school, because good nutrition then becomes ingrained in the child's experience.

Claim of policy argues that certain conditions should exist, or that something should or should not be done, in order to solve a problem. Sample claims of policy: Football causes too many injuries and ought to be banned.

Claim of fact makes an assertion about something that can be proved or disproved with factual evidence. Sample claim of fact: Teens who engage in promiscuous, unprotected sex will develop STDs, become pregnant, and/or contract AIDS.
