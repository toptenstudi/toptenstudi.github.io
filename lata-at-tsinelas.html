Tumbang preso, also known as tumba lata or bato lata, is a traditional Filipino children's game. The game involves throwing a slipper at a can or bottle, which one player - the tayà - attempts to guard. The game is usually played in backyards, parks, or in streets when there is little traffic in an area.
