9. what is the 6 change in phase of phisical change of matter


Answer:

The 6 Physical Changes of Matter

Evaporation, Condensation, Freezing, Melting, Sublimation & Deposition.

Explanation:

Evaporation is the process by which water changes from a liquid to a gas or vapor. 

Condensation is the process where water vapor becomes liquid.

Freezing is a phase transition where a liquid turns into a solid when its temperature is lowered below its freezing point.

Melting is a physical process that results in the phase transition of a substance from a solid to a liquid.

Sublimation is the transition of a substance directly from the solid to the gas state, without passing through the liquid state.

Deposition is a gas state directly into solid without passing through a liquid phase.
