1. the lived experience of practical exam of ict students?​


Answer:

Practical exams for ICT students typically involve hands-on tasks that require the application of technical skills and knowledge. This may involve performing tasks on a computer or working with other types of hardware.

Students may be required to troubleshoot errors and solve problems related to programming, networking, or database management.

Additionally, the practical exam may be timed, with students needing to complete the tasks within a set amount of time.

The experience of a practical exam can be challenging and stressful, as students need to perform under pressure and demonstrate their proficiency in the subject matter.

However, it can also be an opportunity for students to showcase their skills and knowledge, and demonstrate their readiness for real-world applications of ICT.
