Answer:

125

Step-by-step explanation:

The exponents tell us how many times we will multiply the number(or the base) by itself. In this case, 3 is the exponent and 5 is the base, which tells us that we will multiply 5 to itself for 3 times.

[tex]5^3 = 5\times5\times5[/tex]

[tex]5\times5\times5 = 125[/tex]
