Step-by-step explanation:

28. 1. Scores in Math of 120 first long quiz for a sample of 10 students are as follows: 84, 75,90, 98, 88, 79,95, 86, 93, and 89. Find the mean score of the students?


MEAN

Problem: Scores in Math of 120 first long quiz for a sample of 10 students are as follows: 84, 75,90, 98, 88, 79,95, 86, 93, and 89. Find the mean score of the students.

SOLUTION:

First of all, identify what are the given.

84, 75,90, 98, 88, 79,95, 86, 93, and 89

Next is to add all of them.

[tex]84+75+90+98+88+79+95+86+93+89[/tex][tex]= 877[/tex]

Then, divide them by the counted numbers of the given.

[tex]877 \div 10[/tex][tex]87.7[/tex]

Therefore, the mean score of the students is 87.7
