1. FOOTBALL FIELDS ARE 100 METERS FROM GOAL LINE TO GOAL LINE. IF IT IS 360 METERS AROUND A FOOTBALL FIELD, HOW WIDE IS THE FIELD?


The field is 80 meters wide.

Length: 100 meters
Perimeter: 360 meters
Width: x

Formula in finding Perimeter: L + L + W + W

[tex]100 + 100 + x + x[/tex]

[tex]200 + 2x[/tex]

Solution: 

[tex]200 + 2x = 360[/tex]

[tex]2x = 360 - 200[/tex]

[tex]2x = 160[/tex]

[tex] \frac{2x}{2} = \frac{160}{2} [/tex]

[tex]80[/tex]
