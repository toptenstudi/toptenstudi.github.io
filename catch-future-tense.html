Present, Past and Future Tenses

» The present tense is the form of verb that helps us to denote things happening in the present or those things that happen on a daily basis.

» The past tense is the form of verb that helps us to denote things that happened in the past.

» The future tense helps us to denote those things that will happen in the future.
