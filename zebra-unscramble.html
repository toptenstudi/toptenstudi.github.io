Answer:

Zebra mares gestate for 11 to 12 months. They usually give birth, or foal, during the night, when there are fewer predators around. The female zebra will typically move herself away from the herd and lie on her side to foal, although some zebra mares may are able to foal while standing.

Unscramble.

1. SSCMULE - MUSCLES

2. ARTHER - HEART

Heart is a hollow muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body.

3. SURETU - UTERUS

Uterus is a female muscular organ that contains the young offsprings before birth.

4. LESTICTES - TESTICLES

Testicle (singular) is a male organ responsible for producing sperm cells.
