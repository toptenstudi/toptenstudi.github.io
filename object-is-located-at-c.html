16. The image of an object as formed by a plane mirror is located ____. a. on the mirror surface b. in front of the mirror surface c. behind the mirror surface d. any of the above, depending on the object's location.


Explanation:

The image of an object as formed by a plane mirror is located depending on the object's location and even the mirror.

The precise image of an object as formed by a plane mirror will depends on the location of the object, the location of the person (if it is a person), and the type of mirror.  In fact, the image location is defined as the location where it seems to every observer as though light is coming from. Since all people see reflected rays of light as they sight at an image in the mirror, then the image location must be the intersection point of these reflected rays.
