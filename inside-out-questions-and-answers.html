If I try to stop my friend right away, my friend won't have the chance to completely sin. On the other hand, if I ignore what he/she is doing, she might repeat the same thing all over again the next time.

I would like to know why my friend was doing it.

Yes. I would ask for the help of our guidance counselor.

I decided to stop my friend right away and help in the best way I can so he/she won't have to do something immoral.
