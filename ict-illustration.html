5. What activities or habits do you practice which illustrate theeffective use of ICT​


Answer:

As a former ICT student, I still practice the proper use of online and computing tools like computers and social media sites. Proper behaviors online and when you use computing resources is known as netiquette. These are the following netiquettes that I am still practicing as of the present:

1. I do not typed in all caps or big letters. Typing with big letters is considered as shouting.

2. I act online as how I act in real life. I am not a keyboard warrior or if I am angry with someone, I do not bring it to social media or any online platforms.

3. I do not support cyberbullying when it happens online. I do not make fun of others online.

4. I only use computing resources during work or school hours.

5. I do not patronize piracy or software that is acquired without licenses.

The mentioned activities above are being practiced by me since then. It is quite effective as I am using computing and online resources for the goodness and I do not cause harm and problems to others.
