8. a boy was able to acccomplish 2000J of work within 20sec. how much power does the boy have​


Answer: 100

Explanation: In classical physics terms, you do work on an object when you exert a force on the object causing it to move some distance. The amount of work you actually do may have little relationship to the amount of effort you apply. For example, if you push on a car stuck in a snow drift, you may exert a lot of force (and effort) but if the car does not budge, you have not done any work! In order for work to be done on an object, the object must move some distance as a result of the force you apply. There are also constraints on the force you apply. Only force exerted in the same direction as the movement of the object result in work. You may think that you do a lot of work if you carry an arm full of books from home to school. In reality you do no work at all! In carrying the stack of books, you exert an upward force to hold the books so they don't fall to the ground. There is no movement associated with this force. As you walk, the motion of the books is horizontal not vertical. Since the force applied to the books is vertical, and the motion is horizontal, you do not do any work on the books.

18. 6. A football field is about 100m long. If it takes a person 20sec to run its length, how fast were they running?


Answer:

To find out how fast the person was running, we can use the formula:

Speed = Distance ÷ Time

In this case, the distance is 100m (the length of the football field) and the time is 20 seconds.

So, plugging these values into the formula, we get:

Speed = 100m ÷ 20s = 5 m/s

Therefore, the person was running at a speed of 5 meters per second.

23. 9. A projectile is fired straight up from ground level with an initial velocity of 256 ft per second, so that its height at any time t is measured in second. during what interval of time will the height of the projectile exceed 960feet? A. 3sec < t > 20sec. B. 5sec < t > 12sec. C. 6sec < t > 10sec. D​


The interval time will the height of the projectile exceed 960 feet height is (C). 6sec < t > 10sec.

Step-by-step explanation:

Is known :

Initial Velocity of Projectile : 256 ft per second

HEIGHT (h) is given = -16t² + 256t

It means projectile need at least 1 seocond to excees 256 feet.

What interval of time projectile will exceed 960 feet?

Calculate Formula :

H = 960 feet, 960 = - -16t³ + 256t

=> –t² + 16t -60 = 0

=> (t – 6) (t - 10) = 0

So the projectile will exceed 960 feet during interval time between 6 second until 10 second.
