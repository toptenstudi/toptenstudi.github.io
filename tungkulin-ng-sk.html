Measure of SKMeasure of KY

[tex]\underline{\sf{Solution:}}[/tex]

2x - 4 = x + 82x - x = 8 + 4x = 12

[tex]\underline{\textsf{Measure of SK:}}[/tex]

2x - 42(12) - 420 cm

[tex]\underline{\textsf{Measure of KY:}}[/tex]

Since KY is congruent to ON, we'll find the measure of ON.ON = KY = 3xKY = 3(12)KY = 36 cm

[tex]\underline{\sf{Answer:}}[/tex]

SK = 20 cmKY = 36 cm
