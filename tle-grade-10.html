10. how to make recipe grade 10 tle cokery


first po ilalagay yung mga ingridients and then the recipeSTEPS ON HOW TO MAKE A RECIPE:

1. Identify styles of cooking you most enjoy.

2. Look through cookbooks, magazines and recipe websites for inspiration

3. Identify why you’ll be adapting the recipe.

4. Make the recipe before adapting it.

5. Know where you can’t make changes to a recipe.

6. Use ratios to avoid mishaps and create more recipes.

7. Learn the ratios for stocks and sauces.

8. Know batter and bread ratios.

9. Study the ratios for custard, crème anglaise and sweet sauces.

10. Take time to consider what would make the recipe better.

11. Know that flavor is not the same as taste.

12. Make your changes to the recipe.

13. Evaluate the modified recipe.

14. Give it a name.

15. List the ingredients next.

16. Write the instructions.

17. Proof, sign and date.

14. reflection in grade 10 TLE 3rd grading about poultry and 4th grading about meat​


I can provide some information about poultry and meat production in general.

Poultry production involves raising domesticated birds such as chickens, ducks, geese, and turkeys for their meat, eggs, or feathers. The process involves providing appropriate housing, feeding, and healthcare to ensure the birds' health and welfare. Poultry farming is an essential industry that provides a significant source of protein for human consumption worldwide.

On the other hand, meat production involves the raising and processing of various animals such as cattle, pigs, sheep, and goats for their meat. The process involves providing appropriate nutrition, housing, and healthcare to ensure the animals' comfort and welfare. Meat production is also an essential industry that provides a significant source of protein for human consumption.

In studying poultry and meat production, it is important to understand the different methods of raising and processing animals to ensure their welfare and the safety and quality of the food products. Additionally, it is crucial to consider the environmental and ethical implications of these industries and explore more sustainable and responsible practices.

In my reflection for the 3rd grading period in Grade 10 TLE, I learned a lot about poultry. I gained knowledge about the different types of poultry, including chickens, ducks, and turkeys, and how to properly care for them. I also learned how to identify the different parts of poultry and how to prepare them for cooking. Through hands-on activities, such as preparing different poultry dishes, I also learned about the different cooking methods that can be used for poultry and how to create different flavors and textures.

In the 4th grading period, I focused on meat. I was able to learn about the different types of meat, such as beef, pork, and lamb, and how to properly store and prepare them.
