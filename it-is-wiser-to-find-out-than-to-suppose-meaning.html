1. it is wiser to find out than to suppose. Mark Twain explain the meaning ​


Explanation:

It is always wiser to find out the truth rather than making assumptions. In today's world, there is an abundance of information available, and it can be easy to fall into the trap of assuming we know something when we don't. However, taking the time to verify information can help us to avoid making foolish mistakes, spreading false information, and making uninformed decisions. The next time you find yourself about to make an assumption, remember Twain's words of wisdom and take the time to find out the truth.
