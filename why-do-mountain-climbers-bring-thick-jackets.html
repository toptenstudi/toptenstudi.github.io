1. why do mountain climbers bring thick jackets


Answer:

Mountain climbers bring thick jackets when they are hiking to protect themselves from the low temperature.

Explanation:

As the altitude increases, the pressure decreases. When there is pressure, there is heat. Since the pressure is decreasing at the top of the mountains, there is lesser heat produces hence temperature drops and the colds can be felt.

Another reason is when air touches the mountain, it rise up and release the cold temperature. Unlike in ground level, when air touches the sea level, the air is distributed hence cold temperature is not felt right away.

It is dangerous having no thick jacket because of the colds. One may experience hypothermia which can deplete oxygen level and can eventually cause death.
