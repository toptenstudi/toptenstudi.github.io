1. what is the similarities of rondalla and pangkat kawayan?


Answer:

Rondalla - is an ensemble of string instruments. The instruments of the rondalla include the banduria, laud, octavina, guitar, and string bass.

Pangkat kawayan - a big ensemble known for using non-traditional bamboo instruments such as: bumbong, kalatok, talunggating, and angklung.
