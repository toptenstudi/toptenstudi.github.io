11. how are you going to assess the maturity of mango fruits​


Answer:

Growers and stores evaluate mango maturity in five stages, according to the color of the flesh, firmness and "degrees Brix," which indicates sugar content. They use tools like a fruit penetrometer to measure firmness, a colorimeter to assess flesh color and a refractometer to get degrees Brix.
