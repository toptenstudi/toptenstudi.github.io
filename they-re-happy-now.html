"The Aged Mother" is a Japanese folktale about a cruel ruler who issues a decree that all old people must be put to death. A farmer and his mother try to escape the decree by fleeing to a distant mountain, where they meet an old woman who helps them. The old woman reveals that she is the spirit of the mountain and gives the farmer a magic paddle that will make him invisible. The farmer uses the paddle to return to his village and save the other elderly people by convincing the ruler to rescind his decree. The story highlights the importance of respecting and caring for the elderly, and the wisdom and guidance they can offer.
