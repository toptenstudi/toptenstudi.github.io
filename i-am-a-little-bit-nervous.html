What is reported speech?Reported speech is a mode of sharing what someone said in the past without having to use the exact words and quotation marks.

How can we convert this sentence into a reported speech?To turn this sentence to a reported speech, we must change the verbs into their past tense forms ("says" becomes said, and "am" becomes "was")
