True or False? 4.A square is also a rectangle. True or False? 5.Two rectangles are formed when you draw a diagonal in a square. True or False?


Answer:

1.True

2. False

3. True

4. False

5. False

Step-by-step explanation:

1. A square is a special case of a rhombus, because it has four equal-length sides and goes above and beyond that to also have four right angles.

2. A trapezoid can never be a parallelogram. Trapezoids have only one pair of parallel sides; parallelograms have two pairs of parallel sides.

3. A square has two pairs of parallel sides, four right angles, and all four sides are equal. It is also a rectangle and a parallelogram. A rhombus is defined as a parallelogram with four equal sides.

4.A rectangle is a quadrilateral, all of whose angles are equal right angles. Square, apart from all equal angles, also has all sides equal.
