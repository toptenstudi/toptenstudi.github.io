1. what is bounce pass in basketball​


Answer:

The pass is thrown to the floor so that it bounces to the intended receiver.

Explanation:

There are essentially two types of passes: Air Pass - The pass travels between players without hitting the floor. Bounce Passes - The pass is thrown to the floor so that it bounces to the intended receiver.


2. why bounce pass, chest pass, in basketball is important?​


Answer:

The bounce pass is frequently used when your on-ball defender has their hands high and you still need to make a pass. The bounce pass to a teammate regularly occurs when making a post-entry pass or a pass from the post back out to a teammate if the post player is double teamed
