3. Maria won a gift certificate worth Php 400.00 and like to share her winnings to the class by buying notebooks for the entire class. The notebooks she would like to buy costs Php 15.25 each. What is the maximum number of notebooks can Maria buy?

ANSWER:

The maximum number of notebooks that Maria can buy is 26.

SOLUTION:

Change the divisor 15.25 to a whole number by moving the decimal point 2 places to the right. Then move the decimal point in the dividend the same, 2 places to the right.

We then have the equations:

40000 ÷ 1525 = 26

and therefore:

400 ÷ 15.25 = 26
