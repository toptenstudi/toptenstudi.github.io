The Story of The Happiest Boy in the World

In the story of The Happiest Boy in the World, the relationship of Julio and Ka Ponso is that of tenant and landlord. Aside from being a landlord, Ka Ponso also became the godfather of Julio's child. As a result, the two have become compadre or friends. Meanwhile, Julio wrote to Ka Ponso to tell that he wants Jose, his son, to study in Manansalay in June. Due to that, Julio asked Ka Ponso if his son can stay in Ka Ponso's house as Jose goes to school. While there, Julio told Ka Ponso that he can make him do anything such as household works.

What Julio Felt While Writing the Letter in the Story of The Happiest Boy in the World

One of the predominant feelings of Julio while writing the letter in the story of The Happiest Boy in the World is fear. In the story, the narrator described that Julio has a vague fear that Ka Ponso will disregard his letter. However, he continued to write in hope. Aside from rejection, Julio also feared for his son. If, in fact, Ka Ponso will accept Jose to his house, the boy might break some things due to him being unaccustomed to the things there.

Characteristics of Ka Ponso and His and Julio's House

Ka Ponso is a kind and compassionate landlord because he let Julio lease one of his properties. As Julio thought about it, he was "lucky enough to have Ka Ponso take him on as a tenant." Furthermore, Ka Ponso also helped in the application of Julio for acquiring land and even talked with the officials related to the matter. Moreover, Ka Ponso offered to become the godfather of Julio's child.

Being a godfather means becoming a friend - not just a landlord - to their family. Julio, in recounting what Ka Ponso already did to them, even added that their family already owed a lot to Ka Ponso for many things. In addition, Julio would not be writing to Ka Ponso this letter if he was not a kind and compassionate landlord.

30. How does Julio feel about jose living in the house of Ka Ponso?


The Happiest Boy in the World

Julio has been very worried about his son Jose living in the house of his compadre Ka Ponso. He was worried about whether Ka Ponso would agree with his decision to send Jose to the town for schooling. He was also worried on how Jose will live his life away from his parents. He knows perfectly that Jose will have to struggle since living away from parents is such a sad experience but he also knew that he will learn a lot from that experience. But Julio is also confident that Jose will be able to make it in the town since he had raised him as a young boy full of courage, good natured, and with a pure heart.
