10. sarah passed her exam.The exam is 50 questions. Sarah got 48% right in the exam. What is she's score?​


Answer:

If Sarah got 48% of the questions right in a 50-question exam, it means she got:

48/100 * 50 = 24 correct answers

Score = (Number of Correct Answers / Total Number of Questions) x 100%

Score = (24 / 50) x 100%

Score = 48%

Therefore, Sarah's score is 48%

Answer:

She got 24 out of 50.

Step-by-step explanation:

base = 50

rate = 48% (0.48)

percentage = ?

percentage = rate x base

percentage = 0.48 x 50

percentage = 24
