1. what is transmission genetics​


The study of the mechanisms involved in the passage of genes from one generation to the next.

DNA Transmission

An organism's DNA is transferred to its offspring through a process called transmission. This word is somewhat synonymous with heredity because it describes how genetic information is passed down through generations.
