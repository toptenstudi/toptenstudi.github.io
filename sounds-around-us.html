Sound also helps us to understand our environment, such as warning us of danger or alerting us to the presence of another person or object. Sound also has the power to influence our emotions, triggering memories, helping us to feel relaxed or energized and so much more. 28. what will happen if all sound waves around us all travel at the same speed?


The speed of sound is not always the same. Remember that sound is a vibration of kinetic energy passed from molecule to molecule. The closer the molecules are to each other and the tighter their bonds, the less time it takes for them to pass the sound to each other and the faster sound can travel.
