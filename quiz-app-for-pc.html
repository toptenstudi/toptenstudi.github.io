15. differentiate all in one pc and deskcop pc


Answer:

The only difference is the number of components. While desktops are comprised of the computer case plus a separate monitor, all-in-ones combine the display and the computer into one package. This consolidation gives the all-in-one computer system a smaller overall profile than a desktop computer system.
