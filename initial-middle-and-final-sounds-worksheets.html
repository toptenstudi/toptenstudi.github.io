Accounting Worksheet Columns Included:

-Unadjusted Trial Balance

-Adjusting Entries

-Adjusted Trial Balance

-Income Statement

-Balance Sheet

Explanations:

Unadjusted Trial Balance

A column contains all the company’s assets, liability, expenses, and revenue accounts, which are used in a concerned year.

Adjusting Entries

All the accounts of the company, which requires the passing of the adjustment entry will get listed in the adjustment column.

Adjusted Trial Balance

The adjusted trial balance will be prepared by combining the entries of the previous two columns, Unadjusted trial balance, and adjustments.

Income Statement

A column contains the values concerning the expenses and revenue accounts only. In this case, if the value of total revenue exceeds the expense column, then the difference will be the net income of the year of the company as it is earning more revenues in the year than what it is spending on its expenses.

Balance Sheet

A column contains the values with respect to the assets, liabilities, and owner’s capital only.

The relationships among the various notes in a musical composition, both as they sound together (vertical texture) and one after another (horizontal texture) 3. Refers to the volume of a sound or note combination of long and short sounds, notes and rest 4. "quality of the sound" or tone color Male lowest type of singing voice 5. Female highest type of singing voice​


Answer:

1. The tempo

2. Harmony

3.Rhythm

4.Bass

5.soprano

Explanation:

1. The tempo of a piece of music is the speed of the underlying beat. Like a heartbeat, it can also be thought of as the 'pulse' of the music. Tempo is measured in BPM, or beats per minute. One beat every second is 60 BPM.

2.Harmony —The simultaneous combination of notes and the ensuing relationships of intervals and chords. Characteristics of Harmony: Chord — Simultaneous combination of tones (typically three or more) that constitute a single block of harmony

3.Rhythm. the flow of music through time; a combination of long and short sounds and silence

4.a bass is the lowest and darkest of the male voices and is ideal for several types of roles

5. For females, the highest voice type is the soprano. In operatic drama, the soprano is almost always the heroine because she projects innocence and youth. Within this category, there are other sub-divisions such as, coloratura soprano, lyric soprano, and dramatic soprano.
