10. Below is a student‘s draft of an introduction for an article about diversity in the Philippines. If a sentence observes proper subject-verb agreement, write C. If it does not, rewrite the sentence. (5) For this reason, tourism experts, along with the Department of Tourism believes that “It‟s more fun in the Philippines!”


Answer:

(5) For this reason, tourism experts, along with the Department of Tourism believes that “It‟s more fun in the Philippines!”

This sentence does not observe the proper subject-verb agreement.  

In this sentence, the subject is plural - 'tourism experts' while the Department of Tourism is just being added, but the main subject of the sentence is 'tourism experts' and the verb used - 'believes' is in 's' form which is a singular form.  

The correct sentence is "For this reason, tourism experts, along with the Department of Tourism believe that “It‟s more fun in the Philippines!”  

Explanation:

Subject-verb agreement rule: Auxiliary verb (be verbs) (is, are, am)

          If the subject is singular, the verb to be used should also be singular in form.  

Example: The man in uniform is dancing gracefully. (man - singular / is - singular)

               The policemen are wearing their official uniforms.  (policemen - plural / are - plural)

Subject-verb agreement rule: regular verb (are those in 's-form' when used as a singular form and without 's' when used as a plural  

               form)  (look - looks)

Example: The lady looks great in her yellow Sunday dress. (lady - singular / looks - singular)

               The ladies look gorgeous with their curly blonde hair. (ladies - plural / look - plural)

19. Explain each of the 7 components of the tourism and hospitality Industry.Travel Industry -stakeholders -Environment -Process and system -Organizational function -Governance -Tourism sector -​


Answer:

Travel Industry - On a basic level, the travel or tourism industry is concerned with services for people who have travelled away from their usual place of residence, for a relatively short period of time. The tourism industry, also known as the travel industry, is linked to the idea of people travelling to other locations, either domestically or internationally, for leisure, social or business purposes.

Stakeholders -A stakeholder in tourism can be an individual person, such as a tourist or a taxi driver. They could be a group of people such as a student group or a family. They could be a company or organisation

Environment -Environmental tourism typically defined as travel to destinations where the flora, fauna, and cultural heritage are the primary attractions. Responsible ecotourism includes programs that minimize the adverse effects of traditional tourism on the natural environment, and enhance . The hospitality sector presents a variety of environmental aspects that, depending on the activity, may have a significant impact on the environment.

Process and system - A group of interrelated, interdependent and interacting three basic elements – tourists, geographical elements (traveller generating region, tourist destination region and transit route region) and tourism industry – that together form a single functional structure .One of the earliest contributions to the research literature on hospitality system was that made by Livingstone and Chang .

Organizational function - The National Professional tourism organizations function in the Philippines are the following: Philippine Travel Agencies Asociation (PTAA), Hotel and Restaurant Association of the Philippines (CHRAP), Tourism Educators of SChools, etc. The five departments that are listed in a hotel organizational structure are Rooms; Food and Beverage; Human Resources; Marketing; and Accounting.

Governance -“Governance is a practice of government that is measurable, that is aimed to effectively direct the tourism sectors at the different levels of government through forms of coordination, collaboration and/or cooperation that are efficient, transparent and subject to accountability, that help to achieve goals of ..

Tourism Sector - Tourism sector is described as an industry with 8 sectors which are; accommodation, adventure tourism and recreation, attractions, events and conferences, food and beverage, tourism services, transportation, and travel trade.

27. In your opinion, which tourism stakeholder is the most important to the tourism and hospitality industry? Justify your answer in 7 sentences.​


Answer:

Communities. The most important tourism stakeholder in tourism that is worth mentioning is the community. In fact, many would argue that this is one of the most important stakeholders in tourism. Communities that are involved in community-based tourism are strong, resilient and extremely willing to show tourists their culture. They are able to manage both the impacts and benefits of tourism, strengthening their self-governance, economic alternatives and traditional ways of life in the process.
