1. 1. What is T-square2. Uses of T-square3. 3 Types of T-square​​


Answer:

1. T-shaped instrument for drawing or testing right angles.

2. T-square is a tool used in technical drawing, primarily as a guide for drawing straight horizontal lines on a drafting table.

3. • Adjustable Head T-square with Ruler - An adjustable T-square to create straight lines at different angles with a ruler attached to the head.

•Regular Fixed Head T-square - A regular fixed head T-square for drawing straight lines. The blade must be perfectly straight in order to draw accurate lines.

•English T-square - An English T-square for drawing straight lines. The narrowing of the bottom blade makes the T-square look as if it is not perpendicular.
