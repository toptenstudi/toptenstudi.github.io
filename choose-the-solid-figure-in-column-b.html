What Is A Solid Figure?

A solid figure is a three-dimensional figure. It is about hollow figures. It has length, width and height. This figure is made up of plane figures connected with each other. Some objects around us may represent different solid figures. Below are some of the types of solid figures and examples of objects that represent them:

Cube - It is a solid figure with 6 congruent square faces, 12 edges and 8 corners. Some examples of the cube are blocks, dice and boxes.

Cylinder - It has 1 curved surface and 2 equal circles on its ends. Some examples of the cylinder are tin cans and rolls of toilet paper.

Sphere - It has only one curved surface. Some examples are balls and marbles.

Triangular Prism - It is a prism that has two congruent triangular faces and three rectangular faces. An example of this is the Toblerone chocolate.

Cone - It is a solid figure with a circle at its base and a curved surface that comes to a point at its top. Some examples are party hats, ice cream cone and volcano.

Square Pyramid - It is a kind of pyramid that has a square at its base. The other faces are the triangular sides that meet at a common vertex. An example of this is the roof of the nipa hut.

Rectangular Prism - It contains six rectangular faces. Some examples are boxes, table and door.

Geometric Shapes

A geometric shape is defined as an area or a figure bounded by lines, curves, and points.

Let's answer the given activity:

1. Ice cube - B. Cube

As defined by the object itself an ice cube is shaped as a cube. A cube is a geometric figure with 6 equal sides and 6 equal faces.

2. Tissue Roll - C. Cylinder

A tissue roll is a cylinder because it matches the geometric property of a cylinderA cylinder is an extruded circle. Thus is made up of two equal circular bases and has no sides.

3. Match box - D. Rectangular Prism

A match box is often times shaped as a rectangular Prism. A rectangular Prism is a geometric figure which is like an extruded rectangle. Thus, it has 2 equal rectangular bases and a pair of 2 equal sides defined by the length and width of the rectangular base. https://brainly.ph/question/107560

4. Volleyball - E. Sphere

A volleyball is shaped as a sphere (in general, All types of balls are spherical in shape)

5. Can of Sardines - - D. Rectangular Prism/ C. Cylinder

I put two answers because locally the our sardine cans are cylinders, but in other countries, sardine cans are shaped as a rectangular prism. Most likey for this module the answer is C. Cylinder

6. Milk Box -  D. Rectangular Prism

7. Volcano - A. Cone

A volcano is ideally in a shape of a cone. Imagine the image of Mayon Volcano and it will show us what a perfect cone looks like. A cone is a geometric figure which only has one circular base and an apex.

8. Toblerone - H. Triangular Prism

A toblerone is in a shape of a triangular prism. Prisms are solid geometric figures which two end faces are equal/ parallelA Triangular Prism has two parallel triangles thus called triangular prism.

9. Bus - D. Rectangular Prism

10. Roof of a Nipa Hut - Rectangular Pyramid

The roof of a nipa hut is shaped as a rectangular prism. A rectangular pyramid has a rectangular base and each of its sides meet at a common point called the apex.
