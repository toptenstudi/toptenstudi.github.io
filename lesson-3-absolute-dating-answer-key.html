13. absolute dating vs relative dating


Answer:

What is absolute dating?

It is the process of determining the age on clear/understandable events that happened in the past in archaeology and geology.

What is relative dating?

It is the science of determining the relative order of events that happened in the past. (In other words... the age of an object compared to something), without determining their exact age.

What is the difference between absolute dating and relative dating?

Relative dating is the age of a rock/rock layer and how many fossils contain compared to other layers.

Absolute dating is the numeric age of a layer of rocks or fossils. Absolute age can be determined/known by using radiometric dating.
