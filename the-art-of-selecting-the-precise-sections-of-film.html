27. 4. It is the art of selecting the precise section of film, then sequencing and joining them to achieve thedirector's desired visual and emotional effect.a. Film directingb. Cinematographyc. Editingd. Acting​


Answer:

The correct answer is c. Editing.

Editing is the process in filmmaking where the precise selection, sequencing, and joining of different sections of film or video footage takes place to create a cohesive visual and emotional narrative. It is an essential part of the filmmaking process that involves assembling various shots, scenes, or sequences in a way that conveys the director's desired storytelling, visual, and emotional effects.

The editor works closely with the director and other members of the filmmaking team to shape the final product, making decisions on which shots or scenes to include, the order in which they are arranged, and how they are transitioned or combined. Through the creative use of cuts, transitions, pacing, and other editing techniques, the editor helps to establish the film's rhythm, tone, and overall impact on the audience.

While film directing, cinematography, and acting are all crucial elements in the filmmaking process, editing plays a unique role in the post-production stage by bringing together the various elements of the film and refining them into a cohesive whole, ultimately influencing the final visual and emotional impact of the film.
