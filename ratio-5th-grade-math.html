12. 3. Your quiz grades are 85, 82, 75 and 90. What must you score on the 5th exam to maintain an average grade of 85? 4.Your grades in Math subject are 87, 88 and 90. What grade do you need on the 4th quarter to get a final grade of 90?please with solution ​


Step-by-step explanation:

3. To maintain an average grade of 85, the sum of your grades for the first four exams must be (85 x 4) = 340.

The sum of your current grades is (85 + 82 + 75 + 90) = 332.

To find out what you need on the 5th exam, you can subtract the sum of your current grades from the total required (340 - 332) = 8.

Therefore, you must score at least 8 points on the 5th exam to maintain an average grade of 85.

4. To get a final grade of 90 in Math, the sum of your grades for all four quarters must be (90 x 4) = 360.

The sum of your current grades is (87 + 88 + 90) = 265.

To find out what grade you need on the 4th quarter, you can subtract the sum of your current grades from the total required (360 - 265) = 95.

Therefore, you need to get a grade of at least 95 in the 4th quarter to get a final grade of 90.

15. Bert got grades of 86, 66, and 91 and 93 in 4 Math tests. What must be the 5th grade so that he gets an average of 90? (with explanation pls)


Answer:

To find the grade Bert needs to get an average of 90, we can use the formula for the average:

Average = (sum of all grades) / (number of grades)

In this case, Bert has taken four math tests, and we know his grades for three of them. Let's call his missing grade "x". Then we can set up an equation:

(86 + 66 + 91 + 93 + x) / 5 = 90

We want to solve for "x". We can start by multiplying both sides by 5 to get rid of the fraction:

86 + 66 + 91 + 93 + x = 450

Next, we can combine the numbers on the left-hand side of the equation:

336 + x = 450

Subtracting 336 from both sides gives:

x = 114

So Bert needs to get a grade of 114 on his fifth math test in order to have an average of 90.
