20. A motor is rated at 1200 W. When it is working at full capacity, how much time does it take to do 2000 J? With Solution.


Explanation:

The power of an object, given the work W and the time t, is

[tex]\boxed{P = \frac{W}{t}}[/tex]

where:

P = power

W = work

t = time

------------------------------------------------------

Solution:

Listing the given values

P = 1200 W

W = 2000 J

[tex]\\[/tex]

Solving for the time

[tex]P = \dfrac{W}{t}[/tex]

[tex]t = \dfrac{W}{P}[/tex]

[tex]t = \dfrac{\text{2000 J}}{\text{1200 W}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{t = \text{1.67 s}}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

Therefore, the time taken to do 2000 J of work is 1.67 s.
