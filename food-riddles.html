24. riddle me this riddles


RiddleIt is a tricky question that has telling the characteristic of an object by using metaphoric words and you are trying to guess it, of what it is. It is also presented as a brain active game.

These are the Examples of Riddles:

Riddle: It has many holes, but still holds water.

Answer: A  sponge.

Explanations: Because a sponge has many holes in it, and it can holds water by absorbing it.

Riddle: How many letters are there in the English alphabet?

Answer: "18" because 3 in ‘the’ and 7 in ‘English,’ and also 8 in ‘alphabet.’

Explanations: It literally the letters of the word "The English Alphabet"

Riddle: What answer can you never answer yes to?

Answer: Are you asleep yet?

Explanations: Because you are sleeping, how can you answer if you were asleep.

Riddle: Where can you find cities, towns, shops, and streets but no people?

Answer: A map

Explanations: Because a map has the locations of everything, but there is no specific living things in it.

Riddle: David’s parents have three sons: Snap, Crackle, and what’s the name of the third son?

Answer: David

Explanations: Because it is said in the beginning, "David's parent" so he is also one of the three sons.

Riddle: What has legs, but doesn’t walk?

Answer: A table

Explanations: A table has four legs, but this cannot walk.

Riddle: What has words, but never speaks?

Answer: A book

Explanations: A book has many words on it, but this can never speak. Because it is an object, it has no lips on it.

Riddle: What is the end of everything?

Answer: The letter “g”

Explanations: It is literally the word "everything."

Riddle: I follow you all the time and copy your every move, but you can’t touch me or catch me. What am I?

Answer: Your shadow

Explanations: Because once their is a light, it will create shadow. And shadow can also copy your every move, because your shadow is literally you.

Riddle: What is black when it’s clean and white when it’s dirty?

Answer: A chalkboard

Explanations: Because chalkboard is literally dark green or black and once a black thing has a white on it, this can serve as a dirty.
