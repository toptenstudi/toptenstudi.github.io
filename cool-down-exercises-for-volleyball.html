15. List down the different equipment in Basketball and Volleyball.​


Answer:

Volley Ball Shoes

Explanation:

Volleyball-specific shoes have a gum rubber sole. This type of sole provides a lot more traction than a regular sport shoe or cross-trainer. Volleyball shoes are also made to support a player's lateral movement. The front of the shoe has extra cushioning and padding to help absorb some of the shock created by jumping and landing. Beach volleyball players can get away with no shoes, playing barefoot.

25. list down the officials of the volleyball game and explain their functions.​


Answer:

Line Judge-If only two line judges are used, they stand at the corner of the endline that is closest to the right hand of each referee, diagonally from the corner.The line judges watch the endline and sideline of their respective corners

Score keeper- Main job is  to make sure  the score is correct at all time. The score keeper uses a score paper to keep track of the game.

Assistant scorer- The assistant scorer's main function is to record libero replacements on to a libero tracking paper.
