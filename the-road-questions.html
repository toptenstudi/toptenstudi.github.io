Answer:

1. Traffic signs provide valuable information to drivers and other road users. They represent rules that are in place to keep you safe, and help to communicate messages to drivers and pedestrians that can maintain order and reduce accidents. Neglecting them can be dangerous.

2. These markers also allow drivers to know their required speed limit on certain points. They will also tell you where and when to turn so that you will not hit any vehicle from the opposing direction.

3.Vehicle-pedestrian accidents – When a driver fails to obey a traffic sign such as a stop or yield sign or a red light signal, he/she can very easily strike and injure pedestrians who are lawfully crossing the intersection or walking near the roadway.

Explanation:

The biggest reason for pouring salt on icy roads is that salt lowers the freezing point of water. Water normally freezes at 32 degrees Fahrenheit, but when you add salt, that threshold drops to 20 degrees (or even 2 degrees) Fahrenheit
