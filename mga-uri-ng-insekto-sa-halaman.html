18. ano ang ginagawa ng insekto sa mga halaman pag dumadapo ano po yung ginagawa pag dumadapo sa halaman


Sila ay kukuha ng makakain sa halaman na dinapuan nila

Answer: Pollination

Explanation:

Pollination is the transfer of pollen from an anther of a plant to the stigma of a plant, later enabling fertilization and the production of seeds, most often by an animal or by the wind.

Entomophily or insect pollination is a form of pollination whereby pollen of plants, especially but not only of flowering plants, is distributed by insects. To make it simple, many insects eat pollen. In the process of eating they become covered in it. Pollination happens when the pollen feeder transfers the pollen to the pollen receivers of the same plant, or another plant of the same species, as the insect looks for more pollen to eat.
