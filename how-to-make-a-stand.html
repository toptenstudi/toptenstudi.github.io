16. Research the steps on how to make a stand in argument/topic​


Answer:

1. Choose a Topic: Identify the main theme or topic you want to discuss.

2. Research and Gather Evidence: Gather facts, statistics, quotes, and other evidence to support your position.

3. Create an Outline: Outline your argument to help organize your thoughts and focus on the main points.

4. Craft Your Argument: Write a clear and concise argument that states your position and the reasons why.

5. Address Opposing Arguments: Take the time to consider potential opposing arguments and prepare responses to them.

6. Use Logical Reasoning: Use logic and reason to support your argument and refute any opposing arguments.

7. Present Your Argument: Present your argument in a clear and organized manner.

8. Prepare for a Debate: If you anticipate a debate, be prepared to defend your argument.
