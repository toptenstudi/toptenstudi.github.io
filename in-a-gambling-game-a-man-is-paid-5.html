21. Solve for the mean and variation of a gambling game where it is possible to lose 500, break even, win 400, or win 700 each time plays​


Answer:

the mean of -500, 0, 400, 700. The mean is the sum divided by 4: 600/4=150.

The variance (variation) is obtained by averaging the sum the squares of the differences between these and the mean:

(650^2+150^2+250^2+550^2)/4=202500. The standard deviation is the square root of this=450.
