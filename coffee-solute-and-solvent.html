25. cup of coffee (caffeine and water) Solute or solvent? ​


Answer:

solute

Explanation:

Coffee grounds (inert solid) contain caffeine molecules (solute). The coffee grounds are submerged in water (solvent) to obtain the caffeine molecules. In water, the coffee grounds are insoluble while the caffeine molecules are soluble. Over time the caffeine molecules dissolve and diffuse into the water.
